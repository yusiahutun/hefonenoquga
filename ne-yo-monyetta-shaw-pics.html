Ne-Yo &amp; Monyetta Shaw: PICS Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-06-11

Ne-Yo has been topping the charts and swooning us all ever since his sappy major 2006 hit “So Sick.” The R&B artist signed with Def Jam’s label and went on to make more successful love songs like “Miss Independent,” “Lazy Love,” and “Let Me Love You.”  

Though, while the singer may be all lovey dovey in his music, he is not all that open about love and dating in his real life. While the 40 year old has always been quite private about his love life and family, we do know that he does have quite the family history. 

In case you didn’t know, the three-time Grammy winner has actually been married (twice!). He currently lives with his wife Crystal Smith, whom he married back in 2016. And before that, he was with actress Monyetta Shaw for about four years before calling it quits. The stars have two children (Madilyn Grace Smith andMason Evan Smith) together.
