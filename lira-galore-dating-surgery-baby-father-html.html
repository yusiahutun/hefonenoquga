Does Lira Galore Have a Boyfriend?

More on Lira Galore

Apart from her relationships, Lira has been bashed for having surgery to reconstruct her body parts. Although the Instagram model continuously denies such allegations, trolls don't get tired of accusing her for going under surgery.

Be it with or without the surgery; no one can deny that the gorgeous model is very beautiful. And through her modeling career, the star sure has a hefty net worth. Even though her exact wealth remains under wraps, Lira probably makes more than the average model's salary of $45000 per year.

Before her fame on Instagram, Lira worked as a stripper and an exotic dancer.

The model was born on 31 August 1993 in Houston, Texas, to African-American parents. Her father, Terrence, abandoned her while she was a small child. So, she was raised by her single mother- Michele, who is the owner and CEO of Wine Fine Forever.

Amidst being raised by a single mother, Lira's life saw a challenging period when she got pregnant at the age of fourteen. Unable to bring the kid into this world so young, she had to go through an abortion.

She opened up about her life before fame on the reality television show Iyanla: Fix My Life. According to Lira, her childhood was messed up because of the absence of a father figure.

Looking on the bright side of things, Lira now is extremely happy with her daughter.
