15. Walking Dead

Many people know The Walking Dead as a television series based on a comic book series with the same title. The series portrays life in the months and years that follow a zombie apocalypse.

Some of the actors on the show are Lauren Cohan, Norman Reedus, Melissa McBride, Christian Serratos, and Josh McDermitt. Multiple game series based on the comics have also been developed and published.

13. Avatar: The Last Airbender

In Avatar: The Last Airbender, a young boy reawakens. He undertakes a dangerous mystic quest to fulfil his destiny as the Avatar. His ultimate goal is to bring peace to the world. The animated series aired for three seasons on the Nicktoons Network and Nickelodeon.

  • Category: American football team
  • Site: Dallas Cowboys

Marvel Studios, LLC is a film and television production company. It is a subsidiary of Walt Disney Studios. It produces the Marvel Cinematic Universe films and series based on characters drawn from the Marvel Comics publication. Its productions are fantastic and have attracted a broad fanbase.

6. Disney

Most people grew up watching and admiring the princes and princesses in Disney productions. This fandom offers a comprehensive database for Disney fans. The entertainment conglomerate's headquarters is the Walt Disney Studios complex in Burbank, United States of America.
