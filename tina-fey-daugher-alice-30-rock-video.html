Like mother, like daughter!

Tina Fey's 7-year-old daughter Alice Richmond made her debut on "30 Rock" last night -- playing a young version of her mom's character, Liz Lemon.

Alice looks just like Tina, doesn't she?! And she's got the patented Liz Lemon eye-roll down pat.

The little girl is Fey's first daughter with husband Jeff Richmond. The couple welcomed their second child, daughter Penelope, in August 2011.
