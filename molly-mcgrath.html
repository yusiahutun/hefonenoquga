Career

After she joined Boston College, Molly got lots of opportunities to make her dream come true. Molly McGrath started working for ESPN as a production assistant in Boston, Massachusetts in 2009. She filmed play-by-play, created highlights, and performed related duties for several ESPN segments.

She was hired by the Boston Celtics in 2011 and given the responsibility of web reporting and in-arena hosting. Molly’s dedication and passion for the assigned responsibilities show in her contribution. Before joining Fox Sports, the California reporter worked for WDHD-TV, NESN, and CSNNE.

Molly McGrath started working for Fox Sports in 2015. She was a reporter for college basketball and football. During the 2016 campaign, Molly worked as a co-host of ESPN College Football Primetime with Mack Brown and Adam Amin.

Molly made brief appearances to showcase Big Ten Super Tuesday college basketball matchups and hosted ESPN/ABC games throughout the 2018 and 2019 seasons.
