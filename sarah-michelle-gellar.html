Sarah Michelle Gellar Biography

Publish date: 2024-02-19

Sarah Michelle Gellar is an actress and producer from America. She is best known for her role in the long-running television series Buffy the Vampire Slayer (1997).

Geller was born in New York City, New York, the United States to Arthur Gellar and Rosellen Gellar. She is the only child of her parents. She was just seven when her parents' divorce and was raised by her mother.

She is a black belt of Tae Kwon Do and was fourth in a competition at Madison Square Garden. Gellar graduated from the Professional Children's School in 1994 as a straight-A student with a 4.0-grade average.

Sarah Michelle Gellar first met Freddie Prinze, Jr. in the set of 1997 teen horror film I Know What You Did Last Summer. They only started dating in 2000 and engaged in April 2001 and married on September 1, 2002, in Mexico.

Prinze and her spouse Gellar have worked together in several movies including 2002 film Scooby-Doo and its sequel, Scooby-Doo 2: Monsters Unleashed. She does not have any extramarital affairs and there is no sign of their divorce too. They have two children: daughter Charlotte Grace who was born in 2009 and son Rocky James was born in 2012. 

Apart from this, Dylan Baker has also appeared in the movie alongside, Patricia Clarkson, Dylan Baker, and Molly Tarlov, the sister of Jessica Tarlov. 

Sarah Michelle Gellar is actively involved in various charities, including breast cancer research, Project Angel Food, Habitat for Humanity and CARE. She has the net worth of $15 million dollars and her salary is $100,000 per episode.
She was nominated and won different awards including People's choice awards in 2014.
