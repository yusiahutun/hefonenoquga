TV NewsJennifer Lopezs Boyfriend Casper Smart to Guest-Star on Her New Show!

Publish date: 2024-03-15

Casper Smart has a new gig — working with his girlfriend, Jennifer Lopez!

The 28-year-old choreographer will reportedly appear on Jennifer’s new show, Shades of Blue as a guest-star during the highly-anticipated series’ first season.

According to [Entertainment Tonight](http://www.etonline.com/tv/175332caspersmartisplayingabadguyonjlonewseriesshadesofblue/)_, Casper will portray a bad guy on the police drama, but unfortunately won’t be in any scenes with Jennifer, 46.

Casper on the Shades of Blue set on Nov. 2, 2015.

While Casper is primarily known as a dancer and choreographer, this isn’t the first time he’s landed a supporting role on TV. He previously guest-starred on The Big Bang Theory and Benched.
