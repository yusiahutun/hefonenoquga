Rihanna Gets '1988' Tattooed on Her Ankle -- Check Out Her New Ink!

Publish date: 2024-09-11

Looks like Rihanna's proud to be an '80s baby!

The pop star took to Instagram on Thursday, to show off the new "1988" tattoo etched on her ankle.

In the photo, the "Diamonds" singer poses with her new ink on display while rocking white Pumas and gold ankle bracelets. "killz Tagged by my dear friend @bangbangnyc," she captioned the photo.

This is just one of the many pieces the Barbadian beauty has added to her collection over the years -- the 27-year-old singer has nearly a dozen other tattoos.

Some of her larger tats include a henna design which covers her entire right hand and the Egyptian goddess, Isis, across her chest.

She also has stars that run down her neck all the way to her back (which matches ex Chris Brown's tattoo), "shh" and "love" on her fingers and gun on her rib cage.
