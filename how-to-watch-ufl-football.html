Ahead of its first season, fans are already eager to find out how to watch UFL football live for free. Created out of a merger between the United States Football League (USFL) and the XFL, this newly formed pro minor league promises a fresh chapter for American football fans across the country—and we’re diving into how to stream all the gridiron action for free up ahead.

The United Football League, abbreviated as the UFL, is a professional American football minor league that plays during the NFL offseason over the spring and early summer. Established in December 2023, this new league represents some of the best players across the Midwest and Southern United States, and comprises teams that were previously a part of the XFL or USFL. With eight teams competing in the current season, there’s no shortage of rivalries to witness in the weeks ahead.

“From day one, our mission has been to expand the game of football and be a league of opportunity, culture and innovation,” Dwayne “The Rock” Johnson, one of the UFL’s owners, said in a statement announcing the formation of the league in December 2023. “As we come together to create the UFL, we can build something powerful, exciting and very cool for football fans—a spring league with lasting impact for all the players with a dream to play pro football and the ‘hardest workers in the room’ mentality to make their dreams come true.”
