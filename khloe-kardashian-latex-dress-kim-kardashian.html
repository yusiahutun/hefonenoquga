Khlo Kardashian Borrows Kim's Latex Nude Dress for a Night Out

Publish date: 2024-08-23

Khloé Kardashian is taking some style inspiration from her big sister Kim.

Last night, the Good American founder headed out for dinner in Los Angeles wearing a skintight latex dress in a blush-beige shade. The maxi dress is a longer iteration of a similar style Kim wore in 2015, the Daily Mail noted.

Khloé even styled the dress similarly, wearing a pair of clear pointed-toe heels that look liked the clear strappy heels Kim wore seven years ago. Other accessories Khloé opted for included a pair of oversized tortoiseshell shades, a small baby pink Fendi handbag, and studded earrings.

BACKGRID

The reality TV star is known for her sultry street style looks. Back in March, she wore an entirely denim-on-denim outfit consisting of a minidress and a sweeping duster coat while attending her Good American x Revolve event.

She previously opened up about starting a size-inclusive business with fashion entrepreneur Emma Grede in a 2016 interview with Harper's BAZAAR.

"Emma and I created Good American because we wanted jeans that can fit real women—and we really feel like this has been lacking in the market," Khloé said. "Our denim line will go from size 0 to size 24, but we don't consider this a plus-sized line—we consider this a line for the everyday woman. We believe in embracing a woman's curves and I feel like now so many people are breaking down these barriers of not only going to a size 6 or 8, which is considered 'normal.'"
