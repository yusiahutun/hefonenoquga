OnlyFans 2022 rich list - 30 top earning celebs from Katie Price to Mia Khalifa

Publish date: 2024-02-19

OnlyFans has seen a rocketing number of content creators and users in recent years.

The subscription platform was catapulted to popularity during the Covid-19 pandemic by sex workers utilising the site during a difficult time for the industry.

But now, as more and more people report huge earnings by sharing saucy content on the site, even celebrities have started to join.

This year alone, stars including Baywatch's Carmen Electra and Donna D'Errico, as well as Charlie Sheen's daughter Sami and his ex-wife Denise Richards, have all joined the platform.

Other celebs to have made headlines for their OnlyFans accounts include reality star Katie Price, UFC's Paige VanZant, and Mötley Crüe’s drummer and Pamela Anderson’s ex Tommy Lee.

According to research by the team at BonusInsider, celebrities have been earning huge amounts of money on the platform this year. Here's who the highest earners are.

Bhad Bhabie

According to BonusInsider, the highest earning celebrity on OnlyFans in 2022 is Bhad Bhabie.

The rapper, who shot to fame after her dramatic appearance on Dr Phil back in 2016 quickly went viral, is one of the most successful creators on the OnlyFans platform.

She launched her account just a week after turning 18 and made headlines claiming to have earned $1million in her first six hours on the platform.

And it seems that she has continued to be successful on the adult content site, with BonusInsider estimating her monthly earnings at a whopping $25million.

Mia Khalifa

The second-highest earning celebrity on OnlyFans is Mia Khalifa.

Her spot on the high earners list is unlikely to come as a surprise to many, with Mia first rising to fame as an adult movie star.

Since retiring from the industry she's continued to have a strong social media presence, with millions of followers on Instagram, YouTube and Twitch.

Now, she's growing in popularity on OnlyFans too, where she posts daily content, and BonusInsider estimate her earnings at a huge $21.27million a month.

Blac Chyna

Taking third place on the list of highest celebrity earners on OnlyFans is Blac Chyna.

The American model and socialite was the top earner on OnlyFans in 2021, reported to have raked in $240million in a year.

But now she seems to have lost the top spot and BonusInsider estimate that she makes slightly less than Mia Khalifa at $21.11million a month.

Top 30 highest earning celebs on OnlyFans

As well as the stars who are in the top three highest earners on OnlyFans this year, BonusInsider also revealed the list of the 30 highest celebrity earners.

Among them are Love Island's Megan Barton-Hanson, singer Pia Mia and British influencer Chloe Khan.

Katie Price also made it onto the list. The former glamour model joined OnlyFans earlier this year and is known for slashing her prices, but BonusInsider think that she could be raking in a massive $2.5million (£2.24million) a month on the platform.
