Obsession: Dirty Thirty by Diesel

Publish date: 2024-01-29

Hip denim label Diesel is celebrating its 30th anniversary this month by releasing Dirty Thirty, two pairs of limited-edition jeans, one for men and the other for women. A total of 1,000 pairs will be available at Diesel boutiques across the city on Friday only.

The jeans have special features including a back patch marked with an xXx (sic) sign in Roman numerals. The best news? The jeans costs HK$390 each, far cheaper than the average pair of Diesel jeans.

During the day the stores will also be presenting special giveaways to shoppers. Just remember to get in the queue early as these jeans are sure to become collectibles. From 10am at all five boutiques including Pacific House, 20 Queen's Road Central, tel: 2525 0540.
