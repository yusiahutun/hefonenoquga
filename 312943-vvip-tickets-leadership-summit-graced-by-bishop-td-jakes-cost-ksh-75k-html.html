Nairobi: VVIP tickets for leadership summit graced by Bishop TD Jakes to cost KSh 75k

Publish date: 2024-07-13

- SOAR leadership summit begins on August 5 and will run for two days

- There are three ticket categories from regular to VVIP

- Holders of VVIP tickets are entitled to an exclusive dinner and chat with speakers Jakes and Masiyiwa

Renowned bishop TD Jakes and Zimbabwean billionaire Strive Masiyiwa will headline the 2019 SOAR Africa leadership summit slated for September 5 and September 6 in Nairobi.

Individuals looking to have exclusive dinner at Villa Rosa Kempinski, Nairobi with the two speakers will, however, have to part with a whopping KSh 75,000 for the VVIP ticket.

There will also be an opportunity for the attendees to interact with Jakes and Masiyiwa during a one-on-one chat and set the tone for the forum.

There are other three ticket categories that go between KSh 15,000 to KSh 50,000 per head.

Regular tickets for young aspiring entrepreneurs go for KSh 15,000 each whereas delegate will be charged KSh 25,000 per head.

Individual VIP tickets are retailing at KSh 50,000 each while group or corporate booking is being sold at KSh 475,000; a group must not exceed 10 persons.

SOAR Africa is held in the continent and it aims to create a progressive platform where leaders and entrepreneurs have conversations that inspire economic progress for the continent.

"We create a space where we champion the right leadership ingredients, share learnings, knowledge transfer and mentorship this is because we believe that only by pulling together will we ensure none is left behind," the organisers said.

It majorly targets business leaders, the civil society, religious leaders, people of influence, government and the political community.
