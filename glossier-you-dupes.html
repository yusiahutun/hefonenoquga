Glossier You is one of Glossier’s most popular products—and one of the most coveted perfumes around. When it was released in 2017, it quickly became everyone’s signature scent, and it was even voted Fragrance of the Year in 2018. Glossier You is described as warm and spicy, with notes of pink pepper, iris, and amber. While other perfumes dominate your natural scent, Glossier You enhances it. And even though the perfume smells different on everyone, TikTokers have found Glossier You dupes that they swear smell just like the real thing.

What makes Glossier You so special? It’s made with more base notes—and fewer top notes—than other perfumes. So it complements the way you smell, rather than covering it up. TikTokers describe Glossier You as “the essence of clean and sexy musk,” and they say it lives up to its promise. The fragrance notes really do blend with the oils in your skin to make the perfume smell different on you than it does on anyone else. While Glossier You has easily recognizable scent, its fragrance notes smell differently depending on the wearer. “You are the last ingredient,” Glossier emphasizes—and honestly, the brand is right.

When something becomes as popular as Glossier You, it’s only natural to look for alternatives. Glossier You is expensive. (A 50-milliliter bottle costs $72, and a 100-milliliter bottle costs $104.) And that’s a lot to spend on perfume—no matter how good it smells.

Amidst the Glossier You dupe craze, one TikToker claimed to have found a men’s body wash that smells identical to You. As a result, Harry’s Fig Body Wash became an overnight sensation—with TikTok users running to their nearest Target to get their hands on it. And that’s not the only Glossier You dupe you can find. Super-fans may argue there’s nothing like the original, but the internet is full of budget-friendly Glossier You alternatives that supposedly smell just like the real thing.

  • Glossier You Eau de Parfum 

    Glossier You Eau de Parfum

    Glossier You is the control of our science experiment. It has notes of pink pepper, iris, and ambrox (which is like amber). Although the iris notes lend the perfume a floral scent, it definitely leans more spicy. Glossier You fans claim that it smells different on everyone, so its notes are more understated for that reason.

  • Harry's Fig Body Wash

    Harry’s Fig Body Wash

    Although Glossier You doesn’t have fig, TikTok users claim Harry’s Fig Body Wash smells similar. It might be worth the try if you’re looking for a new body wash, especially if you can snag it before it sells out.

  • MIX:BAR Cloud Musk Eau de Parfum

    MIX:BAR Cloud Musk Eau de Parfum

    Glossier fans say that the MIX:BAR Cloud Musk Eau de Parfum is a great, cheaper alternative to Glossier You. It also has notes of iris, which is exactly why so many people claim it’s a good dupe.

  • Gourmand Eau de Parfum in Fleur de Blonde

  • Moi? Montagne Parfums

    Moi? Montagne Parfums

    Moi? by Montagne Parfums is directly inspired by Glossier You. It has the exact same notes: iris, ambrette, and pink pepper. This is definitely one of the most similar options on the list, and it’s only $40.

  • Not A Perfume Juliette Has a Gun

    Not A Perfume Juliette Has a Gun

    Although Not A Perfume is more expensive than Glossier You, it still earned a spot on the list because of how many You fans claim it’s very similar. Sephora puts it in the same warm and spicy category as You—not to mention, it also has notes of amber.

  • Fleur de Peau Diptyque

    Fleur de Peau Diptyque

    Another more expensive alternative, Fleur de Peau from Diptyque also has notes of iris and ambrette. If you’re a big Glossier You fan and want to splurge on a similar scent, this might be worth picking up.
