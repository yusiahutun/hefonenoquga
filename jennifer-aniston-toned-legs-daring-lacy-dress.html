Jennifer Aniston displays toned legs as she wows in lacy dress

Publish date: 2024-09-20

Friends star Jennifer Aniston attended the premiere of Murder Mystery 2

    Jennifer Aniston is always at the top of her fashion game and the actress looked unbelievably glam as she stepped out for the premiere of .

    The 54-year-old rocked an incredible outfit with the main showpiece being her metallic mini dress that carried plenty of lace detailing. The silver outfit suited the Friends star perfectly with plenty of daring cut-out sections and a low hemline that showed off her gym-honed legs. She completed her look with a pair of strappy heels and a dazzlingly large ring.

    Her hair was swept to the side with several strands falling down in front of her eyes, as she peeked over her shoulder for the smouldering snap.

    The film is a sequel to 2019's Netflix hit Murder Mystery, which saw Jennifer play Audrey Spitz a murder-mystery obsessed hairdresser who gets caught up in a real murder alongside her husband, Nick, who is played by Adam Sandler.
