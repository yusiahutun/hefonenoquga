Walmart heiress Nancy Walton Laurie and daughter Paige celebrate New Year in Sydney

Publish date: 2024-02-17

Nancy Walton Laurie, heiress to the Walmart fortune, celebrated New Year's in Sydney aboard her $200 million super-yacht, Secret.

Joined by her daughter Paige Laurie, 32, the 63-year-old peroxide blonde matriarch enjoyed last night's fireworks from a prime position off Point Piper, before attending a New Year's Day bash at Catalina today.

Nancy, daughter of Walmart co-founder Bud Walton and worth an estimated $4.7 billion, was seen having an animated conversation aboard Secret as she enjoyed the beautiful Australian sunshine.
