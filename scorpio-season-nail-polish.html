The Best Nail Polish Colors for Scorpio Season 2021

Publish date: 2024-09-13

As cooler fall temperatures begin to settle in, we welcome Scorpio season — and Halloween.

But just because this tends to be the spookier part of the year, doesn't mean you have anything to be afraid of when it comes to what's written in the stars for you. In fact, it's actually time to be a little more bold.

Since we'll be embracing our edgier sentiments from October 23 to November 21, it's time for us to adorn our paws with stylish hues that adhere to the seasonal undertow.

Discover the Scorpio season shade for your zodiac sign below.

Scorpio: Deborah Lippmann Single Ladies

Face the facts, Scorpio: You like to get deep, which is why you'll be inclined to wear this burgundy nail polish on your pincers. It'll remind you not to hurt those you care about, but to connect on an extreme level with love, empathy, and loyalty more than anything else.

Sagittarius: JinSoon Farrago

You like nail polishes that have a little extra flair to it, which is why you'll vibe with this navy-gold sparkly shade. Not only will it make your nails pop every time you wear it, but this nail polish is going to add more pizzazz to any look you're rocking.

Capricorn: Orly Celeste-Teal

A dark green hue will remind you that it's time to plant seeds and attain greatness in the months leading up to your birthday. After all, you're the go-getter and builder of the zodiac. That's why this color will help you to manifest your desires throughout the month ahead.

Aquarius: Butter London Brolly

Although you don't have a plain Jane persona, you'll find that this staple color adds a lot of oomph to your makeup look and is unique in its own way. Navy is the new black, and will make you look sophisticated amongst your peer group as well as at the office.

Pisces: Essie Winning Streak

Being a dreamer by nature, you're drawn to colors that will help make your visions a reality. A light smoky purple shade will help you run towards your innermost sentiments and goals with ease over the next thirty days. Plus, it'll calm your emotional stresses while going through the process.

Aries: Butter London Cotton Buds

The month ahead is extremely transformative in allowing and helping you to start fresh on a clean slate. It's a purifying time that will change your life for the better. Therefore, you'll need to wear a shade of nail polish (like white) that will inspire you to move towards newness.

Taurus: Orly Love At Frost Sight

A shimmery gunmetal grey is the ideal color for you to wear during Scorpio season, as it will add more balance to your life at the moment. Also, you'll feel as though anything goes with this hue of grey, which will make your life easier when dressing up or down.

Gemini: Côte No.78

The next thirty days will have you running around, trying to get errands and work taken care of. But even if it takes more time than anticipated, you'll be able to do it. A deep purple will bring you luck into all of the day-to-day activities that you take on.

Cancer: Sundays No.17

Romance is in the air this month, which means that your passions are running high. Red is the ultimate color of desire, which is why you will want to adorn your claws with this vibrant hue that ignites your heart. It'll attract a new partnership or deepen an existing one.

Leo: ILNP Abundance

You aren't basic, which is why this metallic hue will spark your creative and artistic side. And it will bring a lot of flair to your style this month. You'll turn heads and be the one who sets the trends for your friend group when they admire your nail polish.

Virgo: Butter London All Hail the Queen

This shade of pearly beige is anything but basic. Not only will it blend with your Halloeeen costume and work clothes, it'll prove to be your new fave color. This nail polish will become your new tried and true shade for months and years to come, not just this season.

Libra: Orly Mind Over Matter

You like finding balance in all that you do, so it makes sense that you'll choose to wear a protective shade of black on your nails to bring harmony and peace to your life as we enter mid-autumn. And to ensure that negative vibes bounce right off your aura.
