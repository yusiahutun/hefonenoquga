Pete Davidson Hooked Up With a Friend &amp; Consulted Her About His Penis Size After Ariana Grande Split

Publish date: 2024-08-06

The comedian hooked up with a friend following his split from the pop star, who once claimed he was packing "10 inches."

Pete Davidson wanted a second opinion about the size of his penis -- and he got one.

During a stand-up comedy show at Tarrytown Music Hall in New York over the weekend, the "Saturday Night Live" star reportedly revealed to the crowd that he hooked up with a friend after his split from Ariana Grande, who once told the world he was packing "10 inches."

"I got with my friend who's -- how do I say -- a fast, loose woman?" Davidson joked, according to US Weekly. "We hooked up, and I asked her, 'Okay, what is [the size of my penis] in comparison?"

"She said, 'Well, it's nice, but it's like an average black man's. It's nice, but like the size of an average black dick,'" Davidson recalled. "So I thought, 'Okay, that is white privilege.'"

Grande made the phallic reference on Twitter in June of last year after a fan asked her, "How long is Pete??? Oh, as in the interlude, not as in... Anyway." The pop star replied, "Like 10 inches? Oh, f--k...I mean...a lil over a minute." Grande has since deleted the tweet.

At the time, Davidson seemed unbothered. He even joked about his member two months later during a performance at Auburn University. He described being engaged to Grande as "f--king lit" and said his "dick is forever hard."

Looking back, though, Davidson thinks his ex-fiancée's comment was "mean" but "also genius."

"I don't like that she talked all that shit [about] my penis," he said Saturday, according to PEOPLE. "Everything is huge to her. Why would she tell everyone that I have a huge penis? So that every girl who sees my dick for the rest of my life is disappointed?"
