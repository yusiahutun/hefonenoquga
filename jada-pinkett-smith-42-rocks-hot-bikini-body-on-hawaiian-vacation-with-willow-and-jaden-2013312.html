Publish date: 2024-05-03

Beach bikini babe! Jada Pinkett Smith spent her Thanksgiving weekend in Hawaii, showing off her flawless physique and toned tummy in several bikinis as she frolicked on the beach with her family. 

The Hawthorne star, 42, was joined by her two kids, Willow, 13, and Jaden, 15, although her husband Will Smith did not join in on the fun. Flaunting her impressive body, the mother of two laughed in a pink, tie-dyed two-piece suit with her long, braided locks secured high above her head. She played carelessly in the water and sand with her family, as she confidently walked across the beach in her itsy bitsy bikini.

During another beach outing over the holiday weekend, the Kingdom Come star wore a black bikini top and a tiny zebra-printed bottom with red lining. The star flaunted her muscular legs and toned behind in the barely-there bikini bottom. Pinkett Smith was joined by her lean teenage daughter, who also mix-and-matched her suit in a prism-printed bikini top and and high-waisted red bottoms. Willow, who sported a similar, shaved hairstyle to her mom (with the exception of the teen’s green hair color), also caught some waves as she surfed in the beautiful, blue waters of the Pacific island. The mother-daughter duo also enjoyed a shopping trip while on the island.

Jada’s teenage son Jaden, meanwhile, was spotted on a surfboard with a paddleboard in hand; He was later joined by a group of male pals on a raft.
