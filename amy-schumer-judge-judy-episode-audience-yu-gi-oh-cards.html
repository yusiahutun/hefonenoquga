Amy Schumer Surprises 'Judge Judy' Fans by Chilling in Audience During Dispute Over 'Yu-Gi-Oh!' Card

Publish date: 2024-02-05

Amy Schumer teased her "Judge Judy" set visit back in May, and fans were in for a surprise this past Thursday when they saw the famous comedian watching the drama unfold inside the TV court room.

Schumer was hard to miss as she sat behind the defendant asa two gentleman went head-to-head over a deal over "Yu-Gi-Oh!" cards, an extension of a Japanese manga series about gaming.

Schumer confirmed the appearance on her Instagram page as well, sharing a screen grab of her in the audience.

"Hell yeah! You know I was in the audience on #judgejudy," Schumer captioned the photo. "My sister and I sat in on the cases for the day because we love her!!!!!"

The "Snatched" star also had a little fun on set, sharing a clip of her sitting behind Judge Judy's desk. In a similar post that she shared in May, Schumer and her sister, Kim Caramele, got into character as they posed as a plaintiff and defendant.

Of course, not everyone was aware that Schumer was going to appear on the episode, and reaction on social media from fans who were simply watching their daily dose of courtroom dramatics was quite hysterical.
