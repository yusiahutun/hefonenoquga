Monique Green Bio, Age, Height, Boyfriend, Net Worth, Wiki

Publish date: 2024-06-23

Monique Green is an American actress. She is currently in the spotlight with her major role in the Disney+ series Big Shot. Her acting skills and her personality were a perfect combination in bringing her character ‘Olive’ to life.

Early Life

Monique Green was born on December 23, 1995, in Atlanta, Georgia. Her mother is Gwendolyn Osborne-Smith, the famous model on The Price Is Right. Her Stepfather is Kenny Smith, a retired NBA player. She has 4 siblings, KJ Smith, Kayla Brianna Smith, Malloy Smith, and London Smith. Monique likes to read and travel and also loves singing because she often shares singing clips on her Instagram. She majored in Psychology with a double minor in Mandarin and Asian Pacific studies.

Monique Green Career

Many may not have known her before and may think she is a newcomer, but she is not. She has done many roles. They were mostly minor but her incredible acting skills were noticeable. She made her debut with the TV series ‘Criminal Minds’ in 2011. In 2012, she played the role of Camille Green in the short film ‘The Sum of Loss’. And later she played roles in “Marriage of Lies”, “How To Get Away With Murder”, “Fresh Off The Boat” and “Road to Redemption”.

In the year 2017, she had her busy year. She played the role of Julianna in ‘The Wrong Nanny’, as Kara in “Fuller House” and as Naya Black in “Libby and Malcolm”. After that she played roles in a few more TV series like “Code Black”, “This is Us”, “I Am the Night” and “Black-ish”. Along with these, one of the most notable appearances is the tv series “Meet The Smith’s”, where she appears as herself along with her family.  Over the years she also had a minor appearance in the TV series “The Closer”, “The Fosters”, and “Five Points”.

Her current appearance in the Disney+ series Big Shot is rocketing her popularity and career with her first major performance. Green, when asked about her future projects in an interview with Regard Magazine, said that she has many performances for us but her lips are sealed. We may have to wait and see what spectacular performance she brings next.

Social Media

Green uses social media platforms to connect with her fans like others. Though she has both Twitter and Instagram, she is most active on her Instagram under the handle @moniquegreenofficial. Her Instagram account is verified whereas her Twitter account is not. Green also loves singing and has a highlight section on her Instagram profile with her singing clips. She has a huge fan base of 10.3k on her Instagram. Her Twitter has around 1k followers.
