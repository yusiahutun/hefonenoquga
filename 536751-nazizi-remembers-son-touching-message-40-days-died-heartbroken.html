Nazizi Remembers Son with Touching Message 40 Days after He Died: &quot;Heartbroken&quot;

Publish date: 2024-04-22
  • Nazizi Hirji lost her secondborn child on December 25, 2023, at a hotel in Tanzania during a family vacation
  • The grieving mother, who went mute after her son's death, posted an emotional post, remembering the little boy
  • Netizens, among them celebrities, condoled with Nazizi, sharing sweet and heartwarming messages

Losing a child is the most painful thing any parent can go through. The grieving and healing process takes ages.

How did Nazizi's son die?

Rapper Nazizi Hirji's three-year-old son, Jazeel Adam, died on December 25, 2023, while holidaying in Tanzania with his family.

A statement issued by Nazizi read:

"It is with profound sorrow and deepest heartache that we confirm the tragic passing of Jazeel on Christmas Day, December 25, in Tanzania. We lost this young soul in an unfortunate accident at a hotel where the family was staying."

The little boy was buried on December 27 in Nairobi in accordance with the Islamic religion. Nazizi urged the public to respect her time as she processed her loss.

"We appeal for your compassion and understanding out of respect for their need for peace in this time of mourning. We kindly request everyone to allow Nazizi and her family the space they need to begin the healing process," the statement read.

Nazizi's touching message to son

The rapper-turned-radio presenter shared a touching post on her Insta Stories, remembering her late second-born child.

"40 days since you left mama @jazeeladam ," she captioned a photo of the little angel.

Jazeel died four months after his birthday, and Nazizi shared a post remembering him.
