How to apply for Kenyan ID online?

Publish date: 2024-04-14

Each country has a national identity card that the people use to identify themselves with as citizens of that particular country. Countries differ, some of them the identity card comes conjoined with the drivers licence and other important identification documents. In Kenya the identity card comes alone. There is a standard method that Kenyans have used to apply for their national identity card since time in memorial. One question that comes or arises with all the technological advancements the whole world as well as Kenya as a country is making is if there a way to apply for Kenyan ID online?

As of now, Kenya does not have a fully comprehensive system for applying for the identity card online. This is not due to lack of trying for Kenya is heading in the right direction. How is this? One may ask. Although we do not have a system for applying for the Id online, the government together with the agencies involved have created certain websites where one can get the forms required when wants to apply for the identity card. One is thus able to download those forms fill the necessary information and finish up the rest of the process manually. This is the beginning of better things to come for now what is left is a system where one can fill the information online then submit it online and then get the document worked on and delivered to the person by a means of his or her choice. This already shows we are almost done with step one of the system.

The government, most specifically the Ministry of immigration and registration of persons has so much pressure to make the process online so that it can be inline with vision 2030 and also be at par with all the registration processes. As step by step this Ministry is making the application of its other documents online. Good examples being: the application of a Kenyan passport, application for a certificate of good conduct and recently application of a driver’s licence. In due time, all the application processes will be online as the track record so far shows a lot has been done but even more is coming.

    How to apply for Kenyan ID

  • You need to have a photocopy of your birth certificate, as well as that of your parents. This is due to the fact that you can leave the originals with the officers that are responsible for making the ID as they may get lost.
  • Form 23. This is the form one requires to fill when applying for the Kenyan ID. This form can be found in any office of a register of persons and all District officers’ offices.
  • Once you have the form 23, you need to fill it then take it to the chief or assistant chief of your area. This is for verification that you are from that area.
  • After the form has been signed by the chief or the assistant chief, you take it to the district officer’s office for it to be scrutinized and verified. This is followed by signing of the form by the District officer.
  • After that, the form goes through the following stages:Fingerprints section- The applicants fingerprints are taken; Attachment of the passport photos.Then form is send to the officers responsible for creating the national ID.
  • One is the issued with a waiting card as the wait for the ID to be made. The ID takes approximately a period of two months to come out.

    The following is the process for a new applicant. Is the process the same when one looses his or her ID? What then is the process for replacing a lost ID.?

    For one to replace their lost ID they need the following:

    • An abstract. One can get this from any police station in Kenya. You get the abstract by reporting the loss of your previous Kenyan identity card
    • A photocopy of your lost identity card if you have one or the identity card number of the lost identity card.
    • A shoulder to head photo(passport photo) that was taken against a white or sky blue background.
    • A hundred shillings to pay for the process.

    The replacement can be done at any Huduma centre in Kenya or district officers’ offices(registers department)
