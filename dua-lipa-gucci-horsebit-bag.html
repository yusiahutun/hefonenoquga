Dua Lipa's Sexiest Accessory Is This Viral Statement Bag

Publish date: 2024-07-13

Dua Lipa clearly knows a thing or two about making a statement. Whether it’s recording this generation’s next dance anthem or wearing fresh-off-the-runway designer pieces, everything the Grammy-winning singer touches turns to gold—or, in this case, silver.

The “Levitating” singer made a striking appearance leaving London’s Chiltern Firehouse in the early morning hours of Friday. She emerged from the luxury hotel and restaurant in an impossibly cool outfit that mixed and matched colors and sartorial genres. This included a long single-breasted coat in a speckled tan-gray hue layered over a rhinestone-embellished white tank top and glossy blood-red croc-embossed pants—three pieces that, at first glance, wouldn’t seem to intuitively fit together but somehow do.

Gucci Horsebit Chain medium shoulder bag

Gucci Horsebit Chain medium shoulder bag

The real showstopper of her outfit, however, was the iridescent silver bag she hugged between one arm and the side of her torso. Behold: Gucci’s horsebit leather shoulder bag. This style made a huge comeback for the Italian fashion house this year, with members of the glitterati often proudly showcasing their own horsebit bags on social media or on the front rows of Fashion Week. (It helps that Gucci reintroduced the bag with eight different colorway options.)

Lipa finished off her look with simple pieces, including classic ivory white pumps and a pair of medium-size hoop earrings.

MEGA//Getty Images

MEGA//Getty Images

The pop star previously talked about developing her own style in a 2017 interview with Bazaar. “I like having one piece that makes a whole outfit and then you can wear everything else that’s quite simple,” she said. “I’ve always loved dressing up. I like it when things look like you’ve just thrown them on and sometimes the things you really do just throw on look the best.”
