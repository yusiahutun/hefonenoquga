Jennifer Lopez Wears a Winter White Sweatsuit for Last-Minute Shopping

Publish date: 2024-04-15

Jennifer Lopez looks stylish at any time in any place, even while running some last-minute holiday errands.

The multi-hyphenate was photographed while she was out and about in LA yesterday, where she was seen finishing up some Christmas shopping. For the outing, the "Waiting for Tonight" singer looked casual yet put together in a set of off-white sweats, comprised of a crewneck sweater that revealed a bit of midriff and drawstring waist pants.

She completed the monochromatic look with matching white sneakers and her signature large gold hoops, while wearing her hair in a low ponytail with her curtain bangs framing her face. The star also sported a bit of bronzed makeup.

The Hustlers actor has been spotted in several elegant on-theme looks this holiday season, including another all-white athleisure set and some enviable winter coats. In addition to solo outings, she has also been preparing for her first Christmas with her husband Ben Affleck, with their blended family even making a group trip to choose a tree.

This festive season caps off a whirlwind year for the newlyweds, who officially tied the knot in a surprise Las Vegas wedding ceremony back in July. They later celebrated their nuptials with a lavish Georgia wedding ceremony in August.

A source previously told Entertainment Tonight that the pair were planning to go "all out" for their first Christmas as a married couple, adding that Lopez "wants this year to be even more special than usual and to make new traditions of their own, both as a family and with Ben."
