Vicki Gunvalson Says She Doesn't Think Ex-Boyfriend Brooks Ayers Has Cancer

Publish date: 2024-10-07

It's the moment we've all been waiting for!

On the final installment of Bravo's "Real Housewives of Orange County" three-part reunion, Vicki Gunvalson finally confessed that she doesn't think hr ex Brooks Ayers has cancer.

All season long, the ladies have questioned Ayers' diagnosis -- from his questionable treatments to his seemingly-phony PET-CT scan, Brooks' cancer has been a hot topic. Now Vicki -- who said earlier in the reunion that she and Ayers have officially split -- is revealing whether or not she thinks he has the disease.

When asked by costar Tamra Judge what her "gut" tells her about the situation, the OG of the OC got extremely candid.

"I don't have any definitive documentation that he doesn't have cancer," she said, before Tamra asked Gunvalson what she really thought. "My gut says he doesn't have it," she finally confessed.

"Why did you drag us all through this?" costar Shannon Beador asked. "This has only happened in the last month ... because I am seeing all this stuff and how he has acted in the last two months," Vicki added.

Tamra continued to grill Vicki, asking when she "figured out" he didn't have cancer.

"I would say probably three months ago that I really questioned him and said to him, 'This is not making sense. Why won't you give me your report?'" she said.

"And he kept telling me, 'You have no right to take the reports because you're going to bring them to the women.' I said I need more proof than what you've given me," she revealed.

Vicki's confession comes on the heels of allegations made by her daughter Briana, alleging that Brooks tried to hit on her when she was pregnant.
