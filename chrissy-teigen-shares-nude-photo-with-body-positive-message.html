Chrissy Teigen shares nude photo with body-positive message after giving birth to her third child

Publish date: 2024-07-29

The Cravings founder shared the touching moment with baby Esti

    In a recent heartwarming Instagram post, Chrissy Teigen, 37, mother of three and Cravings founder, shared a touching moment with her baby girl Esti, basking in the joys of motherhood. 

    Chrissy, who has been open about her struggles with body image, chose to celebrate the beauty of the moment instead of dwelling on her insecurities.

    The intimate photo, featuring the nude TV host and her daughter in the bathtub, was accompanied by a candid caption acknowledging her perceived flaws, but emphasizing that her baby girl is "too perfect" to worry about them.

    Friends and followers were quick to shower the post with love and support, praising the honesty and beauty of the photo.

    Supportive comments from her followers reminded the former model that the so-called "life scars" are testaments to strength, bravery, and the incredible journey of motherhood. 

    Commented one follower: “‘Life scars’ of a beautiful woman and mother. It’s proof that we are strong and brave and good! Wear them all proudly.”

    The cookbook author and her husband John Legend, parents to Luna, 7, and Miles, 4, welcomed their rainbow baby, Esti, on January 13, 2023. 

    The couple has been candid about their fertility journey, including their tragic pregnancy loss in 2020 due to a partial placenta abruption. 

    The former Lip Sync Battle host made the courageous decision to share intimate photos of their time in the hospital after losing their baby boy, Jack.

    Despite receiving mixed reactions from fans, she defended her decision to share the heartwrenching photos, explaining that she wanted to remember these moments the same way she cherishes memories of Luna and Miles.

    After undergoing IVF treatments again, Chrisy announced her fourth pregnancy in August 2022. 

    She shared her excitement and hope in an Instagram post, admitting that she still feels a mix of nerves and joy with each appointment. 

    The post featured a baby bump debut, with Chrissy expressing her gratitude for the happiness that has once again filled their home and hearts.
