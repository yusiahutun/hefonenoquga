Disturbing Tha Peace (DTP)

The record label DTP was founded by Ludacris back in 1998. Although it started as an independent label, today it operates as a subsidiary of Def Jam, which is a division of Universal. Initially, it was an outlet for artists who came from the “Dirty South” such as Ludacris given how difficult it was to get a major label record deal at the time.

Among other things, the label handled matters such as marketing, promotions, creative sponsorship, tours and artist development. On the same year the label was founded it released Ludacris’ independent album Incognegro which sold over 50000 copies all sold by Ludacris from the trunk of his car. Other successful artists that were signed to Ludacris’ label include Bobby Valentino, Field Mob, Shawnna, and Chingy.
