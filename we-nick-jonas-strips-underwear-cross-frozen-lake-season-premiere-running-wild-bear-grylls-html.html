Nick Jonas strips to his underwear on Running Wild With Bear Grylls

Publish date: 2024-07-05

Nick Jonas wasn't afraid to get his hands dirty and his feet (quite literally) wet during his appearance on the season premiere of Running Wild With Bear Grylls.

The episode which aired on Monday night showed the 23-year-old pop star bravely crossing a frozen lake - but not before stripping down to his boxer-briefs.

Nick didn't hesitate before wading into the ice-cold water, and as they traversed the lake he yelled: 'We can do this. We got this. It's all us baby, let's go!'

Afterwards, Nick said: 'I’m freezing right now. My toes are freezing. It’s crazy what happens to your body – the instincts that kick in, and you just have to go, push yourself.'

He added: 'And for me, I was screaming stuff too, I don’t remember what I was screaming but I was trying to muster up any courage I had.

'That was something else. The adrenaline does kick in and for me the only instinct was to tell myself that I’m a champ, that I can do this, that life is awesome.'

But Nick - who was diagnosed with Type I diabetes at the age of 13, grew concerned when he was unable to warm his feet up.

'I’m a touch concerned about my feet, he confessed. 'When you finally get out of the water, all of your feeling starts to come back to your body a bit, except for your fingers or your toes. And all you want to do is get them warm, and try not to panic.'
