Ernest Hemingways Great-Granddaughter, Dree Hemingway, Is Playboys First &quot;Non-Nude&quot; Centerfold

Publish date: 2024-08-13

It's a new era for Playboy.

Dree Hemingway, great-granddaughter of Ernest Hemingway and daughter of actress and former Playmate Mariel Hemingway, is the first star to grace the pages of the "non-nude" magazine.

The actress, 28, looks stunning in the intimate pics, where she shows a little skin in a cream ruffled top and matching underwear. And while she doesn't go full frontal, like former centerfolds, she's still seen in the buff.

"My pictorial captures all of me -- the sexy Dree, the childlike Dree, the funny Dree, the tomboy, the Lolita," she says of her photo spread.

"Playboy's images were so iconic in the 1960s, beyond anything else going on at the time. That's the dream for me, to be a part of that, because so many publications especially in fashion, throw out the same exact story," she adds. "There's nothing to compare this next chapter to. Not to mention, my mother posted for Playboy years ago."

The "While We're Young" star may come from a famous family, but she doesn't let the Hemingway legacy define her. "I'll break down my family in two seconds," she tells the mag. "They're my family. My last name isn't anything but a wow factor. It says nothing about me."

Dree admits that people have a misconception about who she really is.

"I once heard that people thought I was wild and crazy, which is funny," she reveals. "I wear my heart on my sleeve and don't listen to what other people think about me. But with this pictorial, I do want people to see me in a different way than they have."

Snapchat/Instagram model Sarah McDaniel covers what Playboy has dubbed its "historic March 2016" issue, which hits newsstands February 12.
