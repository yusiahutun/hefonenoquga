Duran Duran star Nick Rhodes, 61, steps out with his girlfriend Alexandra Stama, 26,

Publish date: 2024-06-12

Duran Duran star Nick Rhodes was pictured for the first time with his much younger girlfriend Alexandra Stama on Tuesday night. 

The rocker, 61, left Chiltern Firehouse with Alexandra Stama, 26, after attending the Serpentine Summer Party. 

It is a new romance for Nick after his long-term relationship with Italian girlfriend Nefer Suvio, 43, broke down. 

The Girls On Film hitmaker wore a bright green suit over a floral green shirt with neckerchief for the outing.

Alexandra, who is more than 40 year Nick's junior, caught the eye in a leopard print slip dress which clung neatly around her waist.

The beauty wore her dark locks in long flowing curls and finished the look with a bold red lip and heavy black-lined eye makeup. 

Nick has moved on with Alexandra after his split from Nefer Suvio. 

The now exes first began dating in 2012 after meeting in the five-star Hotel de Russie in Rome. 

'Our eyes met across a crowded lobby,' said Nick at the time. 'Her exotic look and sense of style attracted me immediately.' 

The keyboardist married Julie Anne Friedman in 1984, but the two split and filed for divorce in 1992. 

The two have one daughter together 26-year-old Tatjana Lee Orchid. 

Nick remarked in 2013 that the age-gap between the couple is not an issue in their relationship and ten years later the couple are still going strong. 

Joining the couple at this years Serpentine Summer Party was singer Ellie Goulding, model Jourdan Dunn, German actress Diane Kruger and Lady Mary Charteris.
