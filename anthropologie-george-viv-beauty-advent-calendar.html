It’s hard to believe that it’s that time of year again, but holiday advent calendars are hitting stores. We’ve known from years past that the good ones tend to sell out quickly — especially if they’re packed with luxury beauty products — so if you see one you want, whether for yourself or to use as a gift, you probably won’t want to hesitate when it comes to buying.

This year, we’re personally eyeing the 2023 George & Viv Beauty Advent Calendar, which is shoppable at Anthropologie. Aside from loving the cute winter design, it’s packed with samples of stuff we’re already fans of or have been itching to try, whether we’re talking viral TikTok fragrances to highly praised face serums.

Some highlights worth noting? Sunday Riley’s C.E.O. Brightening Vitamin C Gel Cream, Elemis’ Pro-Collagen Cleansing Balm, and Phlur’s Missing Person perfume. They may be miniature, but it’s the best way to test out something new before you commit to full size. Plus, they’re perfect for slipping in your suitcase when traveling.

Reviews for this advent calendar are already positive, with its current rating sitting at at full five stars. “I get this advent calendar every year for my daughters,” one person wrote. “They absolutely look forward to receiving it. Great value for the amount of quality product. Get yours before they sell out!”

Great value may even be an understatement. While Anthrolopolgie says that “this year’s edition features over $350 worth of premium treats and treasures,” the actual price is $88. This could be the best deal of the season.
