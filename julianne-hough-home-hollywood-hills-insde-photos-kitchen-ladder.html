Julianne Hough's stunning kitchen in Hollywood Hills home has unexpected detail - see inside

Publish date: 2024-06-05

Her house is beautiful

    Julianne Hough lives in a beautiful hilltop home and her kitchen alone belongs in a design magazine. 

    The professional dancer gave fans a sneak peek at the culinary space which showcased her impressive sense of home-style.

    Julianne previously revealed details of her home to and proudly said: "I've created a safe, special place where I feel like my true self."
