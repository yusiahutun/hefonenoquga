It's Jennifer Aniston's 55th Birthday, and I'm Shopping Her Top Brands

Publish date: 2024-04-30

Bring out the cake and blow up the balloons! It’s Jennifer Aniston’s 55th birthday, and we’re celebrating the InStyle way: by looking back on the exact go-to fashion brands she’s worn throughout the years. Because, let’s face it, whether she’s dressed in a sophisticated, all-season staple or a sexy, strapless dress, Aniston brings the style heat; she is Rachel Green after all. Steal the birthday girl’s past and present looks by snagging the below pieces, including timeless jeans, comfy kicks, and sparkly bling, starting at $32.   

Shop Aniston’s Go-To Fashion Brands:

Levi’s Ribcage Straight Ankle Jeans

In a 2023 interview with WSJ Magazine, Aniston revealed her go-to denim brand that’ll never go out of style: Levi’s, and I’m in total alignment with her. I’ve owned this 60-percent-off straight-leg style for over five years, and they’re still going strong. With an ankle length, high-waist design, and button-front fastening, they’re too good to pass up, and the booty-enhancing pockets are next level — in other words, they look like they were stolen right off of the Friends set. Shop plus and standard fits, available in sizes 24 to 32. 

Baublebar Mini Alidia Cubic Zirconia Ring

On the final episode of The Ellen DeGeneres Show, Aniston showed off her glitzy Baublebar Mini Alidia cubic zirconia ring, which my mom and I both own. Featuring sparkly baguette stones and gold-toned hardware, it’s hard to ignore. Every time my mom and I step out in it, we get compliments on the accessory, reminding us just how good of a buy it was. In case you need more convincing, Julia Roberts owns the same stunning piece, and it’s available for under $50 right now.

New Balance 327 Trainers 

Being that celebrities like Hailey Bieber, Emily Ratajkowski, Kate Middletoon, and Kourtney Kardashian (just to name a few) wear New Balance kicks, it’s no surprise that Aniston is a fan of the brand, too. Earlier this month, the actress jumped on a plane wearing the athletic brand’s retro 327 sneakers, a ‘70s-inspired shoe with a flared midsole, lace-up closure, and breathable material. Combining a mix of textiles, you can shop the street kicks in colors like green, pink, and cream. But I’d shop for Aniston's exact trainers sooner rather than later; other retailers like Amazon are already sold out of the kicks, and I’m sure these will follow suit.   

Hanky Panky Daily Lace Assorted 5-Pack Original-Rise Thongs

Aniston revealed she was wearing a pair of Hanky Panky undies during a phone interview with pal Sandra Bullock in 2020 — and I’m still not over it. I realized we had more in common than I thought, being that I rely on the same panties on a daily basis (I’m even wearing them as I type this story). The stretchy, soft lace and cotton gusset are next level given that they never ride up, give me wedgies, or slip off. They feel so light, you’ll forget you’re even wearing them, and they’re completely disguised under clothing (yes, even beneath snug leggings). Better yet, this five-pack of assorted colors is currently 55 percent off, ringing in at $11 apiece.  

Ray-Ban 55-Millimeter Aviator Optical Glasses 

Nothing has ever screamed Jennifer Aniston so loudly as a pair of Ray-Ban aviators. Worn that same year, these gold, cool-girl frames give off a vintage aesthetic, while being lightweight and timeless. They pair with just about anything, adding the right amount of retro flair to any look. But don’t get it twisted — you don’t need to have bad vision to rock these babies. Pop in blue light lenses for extra eye protection, keep them for non-prescription purposes, or turn them into sunglasses. 

Rag & Bone Casey Raw-Hem High-Waist Ankle-Flare Jeans

We started with jeans, so it only makes sense to end with them. Aniston wore a pair of Rag & Bone pants during that same iconic phone interview with Bullock, and I get why. Rag & Bone’s denim is timeless, elegant (yes, even for jeans), and worn by Martha Stewart, Jennifer Lopez, Karlie Kloss, Katie Holmes, and Kendall Jenner. 

I can totally see Aniston wearing these on-sale Casey flare jeans, which feature a raw hem, high waist, and booty-lifting back. The ‘90s-coded denim comes in sizes 24 to 32 and would look great with a white tee and red mules.

Celebrate Aniston’s big day by shopping for her above wardrobe staples. I won’t judge you if you snag two, three, or even all of them.
