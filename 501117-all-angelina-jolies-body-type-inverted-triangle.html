What is an inverted triangle body type?

The inverted triangle body type is characterised by broad shoulders or busts that narrow down to the hips. As a result, the body appears like an inverted triangle shape, hence the name.

This body type is characterised by the following features:

  • Busts can range from small to big.
  • Great legs often come with this body shape.
  • Shoulders are noticeably wider than the hip line.
  • Body shape projects a sporty and athletic physique.
  • Little to no waist definition.
  • Hips look straight and flat compared to broad shoulders.
  • The bottom is rather flat.
  • Shoulders may be straight, squared, and strong-looking.
