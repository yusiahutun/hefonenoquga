Addis Ababa University postgraduate program information

Publish date: 2024-04-23

Addis Ababa University is one of the leading higher learning institutions located at the capital of Ethiopia, Addis Ababa. Formerly known as the University College of Addis Ababa, the institution has churned out thousands of graduates since its inception in 1950 and also has notable alumni, among them Meles Zenawi, Isaias Afwerki and Hailemariam Desalegn among many others. The institution has then provided another chance to the graduates to further their education through the Addis Ababa University postgraduate programs made available.The Masters (MSc.) and Doctorate programs (PhD) however do not vary much from the previously made available courses, that also include the eight postgraduate programs that were launched at the institution back in 2015.

There are a number of post graduate programs to choose from in the Addis Ababa University postgraduate programs, a total of more than 221 in all, 74 of which offer Ph.D You might be asking yourself what the entire Addis Ababa University postgraduate program is all about. Well, I have the answer.

Addis Ababa University postgraduate program

The postgraduate program in the Addis Ababa University entails further learning of respective disciplines by the qualified applicants beyond the Bachelors Degree level, in disciplines offered at the University. In other words, it offers the further study and research of disciplines to willing applicants who have to go through a series of requirements to qualify.

The postgraduate programs which can be sorted to fit one’s schedule can be done on full time, which is all day long, everyday. The courses can also be done during evenings and the weekends for those who have not the time through the Addis Ababa University postgraduate extension program which caters for all such types of study.

Since you have the general overview of the idea of the programs, let’s now have how one can apply for the postgraduate program and the application and intake dates for one to get into the Addis Ababa University. One can apply for the postgraduate programs either manually or through the Universities online portal, which offers a link through which the application forms can be downloaded.

The Addis Ababa University Registrar office which deals with applications and registration of courses insists that all applications must be made online before the documents can be submitted to the University premises personally. The university's portal redirects has a button that enables you to access the application forms and requirements before applying to the University.

The general intakes dates for the postgraduate students are all year long. This is to say that one can apply and be admitted into the University at any period of the year. However, learning will begin at the beginning of the subsequent semester, from the day in which your application to the University is approved. The factor that remains constant is the application period for the postgraduate programs. The period which often is in the month of July for different courses is invariable as the years pass.

As per the university announcement, the application requirements for the Addis Ababa University postgraduate students include:

General requirements for PhD applicants

The Doctorate level is about the highest one can reach in the his or her study life. At Addis Ababa University, the general requirements to secure admission for this postgraduate program level are;

  • One must submit an accurate evidence of capacity in research. This could be in the form of Masters thesis, consulting reports or even professional papers to the staff concerned.
  • Applicants must submit a statement of purpose that is between 300 – 500 words long concerning the intent of him or her pursuing the said degree.
  • Must have a standing grade point average that ranges from 3.5 to 4.00 or an equivalent in ECTS. Candidates with points ranging from 3.0 to 3.5 will be picked considering their experience and achievements.
  • Brief curriculum vitae of the applicant shall be placed in the file to show their achievements.
  • Original Transcripts from the university in which the applicant finished their coursework should also be in the file before the application is processed.
  • The candidate must also fulfill the General requirements of admission into the Addis Ababa University.

General requirements for MSc programs

  • For one to be able to pursue a Masters program in Addis Ababa University, you must be able to meet the following requirements:
  • One must possess the original transcripts of the institution at which they took their bachelors degree before the application is processed.
  • The candidate must submit a statement of purpose showing their intent in pursuing the said degree.
  • The applicants can submit letters of recommendation from people who are knowledgeable of the applicant’s academic capabilities disregarding parties that might cause conflicts of interest for example friends and even family members.
  • The candidate must fulfill the general requirements of joining the Addis Ababa University.

In addition to the requirements of the said postgraduate programs and the general requirements of joining the Addis Ababa University, the candidates must also register at least a pass in the Addis Ababa University postgraduate program entrance exam.

The Addis Ababa University postgraduate program registration also occurs annually, between the months of August and September for the various postgraduate courses offered. The Addis Ababa University registration shall also take place within the usual months, that is from August and September.
