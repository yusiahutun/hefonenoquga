Man Sets 23 Strict Rules for Estranged Wife Ahead of Reunion: &quot;Must Sleep in Night Dress&quot;

Publish date: 2024-07-07
  • The man and his wife got married in 2014 and have two children together; they separated years later
  • They are now seeking to rebuild their relationship but the husband drafted an MoU that contains 23 rules which the wife is expected to follow
  • Some of the rules include that she must sleep in a nightdress, apologise to her husband whenever he is angry and must always be available to provide him with conjugal needs

A Ugandan man has laid out 23 stringent conditions that his estranged wife should agree to follow ahead of their reunion.

The man and his wife are said to have tied the knot in 2014 but would later go their separate ways following their differences but they now want to reunite.

However, according to a report by Daily Monitor, the hope of rekindling their marriage after years of separation now faces a major roadblock after the husband spelt out 23 rules that the wife must follow if she is to return to their matrimonial home.

In his draft memorandum of understanding, the man said his wife and mother of two must strictly adhere to his rules which will be reviewed annually to track the progress.

Strict conditions

Some of the rules include:

1. The husband is always right

2. The husband is the master of the family regardless of whether he has a job or money

3. The wife is to always ask permission before doing anything including spending personal money, visiting parents or in-laws, staying out late, even going to bed or turning her face away from her husband in bed, the husband is still the master and has full control on all the operations, even in bed.

4. If there is a quarrel with the husband and then a visitor comes, the wife should smile as if nothing happened.

5. Anytime the wife thinks she is right in a matter, she should refer herself to Article 1 of the agreement that states that the husband is always right.

6. The wife is not to object to the husband’s bodily needs unless for health reasons.

7. A good wife must go to bed in her nightdress with no panties or bra except during monthly flow, which he must know about to allow the husband access her at any time he feels like.

8. The husband doesn’t take orders, only gives them. She is to never shout at him and always apologise for whatever has upset him.

9. A woman must never deny her husband romance except on grounds of health and prayers, even in such scenarios, she must politely and lovingly explain to him.

10. When the husband is shouting, the wife keeps quiet and that is the time to apologise for whatever has upset him, apologising to him after you have shouted and insulted him does not work.
