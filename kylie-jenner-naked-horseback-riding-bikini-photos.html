Kylie Jenner's Kylie Skin Vacay, Naked Horseback Riding Photos

Publish date: 2024-06-17
  • Kylie Jenner is currently on a Kylie Skin-themed vacation in Turks and Caicos with some of her closest friends.
  • In a series of new photos, Jenner can be seen horseback riding while topless and posing in some seriously cute vintage bikinis.
  • It was recently revealed that Jenner is the second-highest paid celebrity of the past year (behind only Taylor Swift), having earned an impressive $170 million.

Kylie Jenner's Instagram posts are usually pretty revealing, so her latest vacation snaps are totally unsurprising.

The Kylie Skin creator continues to share a plethora of updates from Turks and Caicos, where she's been vacationing with a group of friends. And in a totally on-brand move, Jenner went horseback riding while topless. It's just a casual way to spend a beach vacation whilst simultaneously freeing the nipple.

The makeup mogul also rocked a vintage Dior bikini from the brand's Spring 2001 collection, which she acquired from vintage store Treasures of NYC (you can shop similar items online now).

Jenner also donned a seriously tight dress as she walked up a flight of steps.

Earlier in the vacation, the Kylie Cosmetics founder wore an eye-catching vintage Venus de Milo one-piece by Jean Paul Gaultier, which she bought from El Cycèr.

And no one should forget that Jenner posed completely naked during the trip, wearing only a floppy sun hat to preserve her modesty.

It's definitely time to book a vacation, though possibly not one that includes topless horseback riding.
