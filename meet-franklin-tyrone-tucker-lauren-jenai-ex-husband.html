Meet Franklin Tyrone Tucker, CrossFit Co-Founder Lauren Jenai Ex-Husband

So, an insider told PageSix in December 2022 that CrossFit cofounder Lauren Jenai and her husband Franklin Tyrone Tucker have been living apart for over a year (since July 2021) and are expected to divorce after Franklin’s case is resolved.

Also, before that, the couple who had known each other as teenagers had reconnected and then got engaged while Franklin was jailed. It is said that Lauren reached out to Franklin upon hearing about his arrest.

Anyway, the two separated in 2021, i.e., without a divorce. And despite the break, Lauren stood by Franklin’s innocence as he served his sentence.

The whole world witnessed the fitness guru put up the $2 million bail money demanding Franklin’s release in a dramatic fashion. Lauren had actually put up two of her homes to secure the bond, some three years ago. Luckily, in December 2022 that bond deal not only expired, but the judge also issued a new, much lower bond. The trial is now expected to be held in the spring of 2023.

In the meantime, Franklin has been under house arrest since being released on the said $2 million. Until that happened, he was awaiting trial for murder at a Florida detention center.

Franklin Tyrone Tucker Treehouse Murder

A man was stabbed to death on a staircase leading to a tree house on Stock Island on 17 November 2017. One man was found guilty in the case on 2 December 2022. Another man’s trial was still pending in the case. The “another man’ is none other than Franklin Tyrone Tucker.

In the meantime, detectives and prosecutors have been believing that the whole situation, now dubbed locally as the “tree house murder”, was over a “crack cocaine deal gone wrong.”

Franklin, while maintaining his innocence, was bonded out of county jail in 2019. He has since been under house arrest since being released on the $2 million put up by Jenai. Also, Franklin is separately suing police and prosecutors in Monroe County, Florida.
