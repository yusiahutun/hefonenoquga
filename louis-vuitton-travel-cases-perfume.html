Louis Vuitton launches travel cases for your perfume

Publish date: 2024-02-25

Louis Vuitton

Have you ever felt concerned about the fragility of your glass fragrance bottle? Well, Louis Vuitton has devised a luxe solution in the form of its new mini travel cases designed to protect your perfume from harm.

The brand has applied "its savoir-faire and mastery in custom-designing the perfect case for transporting and protecting each item of one's personal belongings", so that you never have to worry about your fragrance being knocked about in your bag. Priced at £330, the leather case can also be monogrammed with your initials for a personal touch.

The item comes in varying shades from dusty millennial pink and cream to the brand's classic logo print. A gold buckle ensures that your fragrance remains extra safe.

Necessary? Perhaps, not. Desirable? Yes. One to keep a note of for when Christmas wish-lists come around.
