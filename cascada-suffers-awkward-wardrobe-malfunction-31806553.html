Cascada suffers awkward wardrobe malfunction as fans wowed by ageless appearance

Publish date: 2024-08-09

Cascada star Natalie Horler suffered a rather awkward wardrobe malfunction as she showed off her ageless beauty on Instagram.

Posing up a storm in a Reel on the social media platform, the Everytime We Touch hitmaker – who partnered with DJs Yanou and DJ Manian to form the trio that was Cascada – performed Mariah Carey’s hit track All I Want for Christmas is You.

She let her white blonde hair cascade in sleek waves down past her shoulders, slipping into a white t-shirt with a red slogan as she glammed up for the lens. Opting for a deep peach lip, delicate brows and chunky winged eyeliner, Natalie’s make-up was where the wardrobe malfunction lay.

It seemed her huge lashes had begun to transfer dark mascara to her upper eyelid, leaving a dark smudge and some of her eyelash glue visible. But it just made people love her more, as one fan gushed: “She’s an international superstar but she’s just like us with mascara on her eyelid!”

Natalie herself responded to the quip, responding: “Cracked me up reading that! Didn’t even notice.” More fans rushed to comment on her incredible voice, with one writing: “Omg you sounded amazing singing this. We need a Christmas album from you now! And new music, your voice is so pretty.”

Somebody else agreed: “You’re incredible!!!” as another fan said: “I hope there’s a new album. But if not, any new songs will do!” Another fan quickly told Cascada she has the “best voice ever”.

Captioning her video on the ‘Gram, Natalie penned: “Throwin’ it back with a little @MariahCarey this time of year… I recorded this clip a few years back – can you even IMAGINE how much new music is coming your way in 2024?

“Oooh it’s gonna be exciting!” She added the hashtags #ExcitingStuffToCome, #StayTuned and #NewMusic.

Later, she wowed in a tiny silk corset to get in the festive spirit. The bright scarlet one-piece was a clinging strapless number, exposing her strong thighs as she added a black Santa hat and arranged her waist-length tresses in a sleek style.

Cascada’s last major hit came in 2009 when she released Evacuate The Dancefloor, which reached number one in the UK. Everytime We Touch soared to a number two position in 2005, while she also had hits with Truly Madly Deeply, What Hurts the Most and Because the Night.

Her music isn’t the only thing that has changed since 2009, as in 2011 Natalie tied the knot with banker Moritz Raffelberg in a private Italian ceremony. In 2015, the pair welcomed daughter Jamie together.
