Nonetheless, it was a very fruitful experience to have won a silver medal in his first attempt. Likewise, he again got selected for his second Olympics, which was the 2016 Rio Olympics.

Further, he competed in the 10-meter synchronized diving platform, the 3-meter diving platform, and the men’s synchronized 3-meter springboard.

Similarly, they also ended up fourth for the 3-meter synchro springboard. This was also a very heartbreaking result for the german athletes and the fans.
