Black Friday—the day after Thanksgiving—started in 1961 in Philadelphia as a phrase for the police to describe the crowds and traffic that would occur on the day after Thanksgiving as many people started Christmas shopping. Since then, Black Friday has evolved into day where stores across the United States open at midnight (or even on Thanksgiving) and offer highly discounted sales. Though Black Friday is known for its massive crowds and doorbuster deals, many of the best sales are online—including for streaming sites.

But back to Peacock’s Black Friday deal for 2023. Read on for what to know about Peacock’s Black Friday deal for 2023 and how to sign up before the promotion ends to save big on one of Hollywood’s most underrated streaming services. You won’t find a deal as cheap as this one for at least another year.

    Does Peacock have a free trial?

    Unfortunately, Peacock does not have a free trial, which is why its Black Friday deal is the closest users will ever get to subscribing for free. Peacock ended its free plan in 2023.

    How much is Peacock?

    Without any deals, Peacock offers two plans: Peacock Premium for $5.99 per month and Peacock Premium Plus for $11.99 per month. Peacock Premium offers more than 80,000 hours of TV shows and movies, including Peacock original series like Poker Face and Real Housewives Ultimate Girls Trip; live sports and events; current NBC and Bravo shows; and 50 live channels. Peacock Premium Plus offers everything included Peacock Premium, as well as no ads; the ability to download and watch select titles offline; and local access to your live NBC channel.
