Meghan Markle's wedding ring was made with a gift from the Queen

Publish date: 2024-03-22

Prince Harry and Meghan Markle have chosen Cleave and Company to make their wedding bands, confirmed Kensington Palace.

Markle's ring is special in particular, as it was made using a piece of Welsh Gold, which was gifted by the Queen.

Prince Harry's ring is a platinum band with a textured finish. Both of their rings were made in the Cleave workshop.

Getty Images

Prince William, the Duke of Cambridge, will carry the rings to St George's Chapel as part of his role as the best man to his brother.
