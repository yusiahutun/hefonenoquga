Daisy Ridley brings the drama to the Star Wars premiere in Vivienne Westwood Couture

Publish date: 2024-03-04

Daisy Ridley looked beautiful for the London premiere of Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker in Leicester Square last night, choosing to wear a dramatic, blue, velvet gown by Vivienne Westwood Couture for the occasion.

The dress featured a fitted corset dress in midnight blue silk velvet with a detachable train in silk tulle degrade. She paired the look with some blue jewels and a cluster of very fitting (and festive) star earrings – and slicked her hair back.

Courtesy of Vivienne Westwood

Karwai Tang//Getty Images

The colour was a perfect fit for the blue carpet and also to match her co-star John Boyega, who wore a bright blue West African ensemble.

Karwai Tang//Getty Images

Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker is the third movie in the series that Ridley has starred in – and the actress recently admitted that director JJ Abrams warned her before signing up just what she was getting herself into.

"This is not a role in a movie. This is a religion for people. It changes things on a level that is inconceivable," she recalled him telling her in an interview with The Guardian.

The film hits cinemas today, Thursday 19 December.
