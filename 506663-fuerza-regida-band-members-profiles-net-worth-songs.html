Fuerza Regida band members

Fuerza Regida is a trap music and Mexican-American band from San Bernardino, California. The band’s heavily pushing forward the urban corrido movement. It consists of lead singer José Ortiz Paz, rhythm guitarist Khrystian Ramos, backing vocalist and guitarist Samuel Jaimez and a sousaphone player named José.
