The 15 most expensive makeup brands in the world in 2021

Publish date: 2024-02-23

Makeup isn’t simply a way to beautify your face. It is a form of self-expression and creativity, too. There used to be a strong stigma against men putting on any form of makeup unless it was a rockstar wearing nail polish. Now, however, there are male makeup artists who not only practice the art but apply it to themselves, too. But which are the most expensive makeup brands in the world?

The most expensive makeup brand in the world has its products ranging as high as $250. Luxury makeup brands like Dior and YSL are marketed by celebrities and social media influencers to get more customers, mainly when they introduce new products in the market.

Most expensive makeup brands

When it comes to makeup, quality makes all the difference. If you want yours to look good, last all day, and be gentle on your skin, you must be careful about what products you use. Here are some of the most expensive makeup brands:

1. La Praire

La Praire is the world's most expensive makeup. The packaging of the products speaks of class, and each makeup box's content comes uniquely styled. One of the things that make the brand stand out is its Caviar-infused foundation. The caviar makes the foundation appear smooth, and you can wear it the whole day.

2. Chanel

Chanel is a big brand that is mainly associated with A list celebrities and rich kids. It is no surprise that their makeup products get to be pricy. Their cosmetic products are as high end as other fashion accessories.

3. Giorgio Armani

This is another brand that is associated with class and luxury. Giorgio Armani cosmetic products can be as expensive as $200. A majority of the stylists and makeup artists use Armani’s eye makeup set with primer and mascara.

4. Dior

Dior is another fashion giant that offers pricy but quality make up. Consumers that use the brand’s products speak highly of their highlighters and eye shadows. The price of the palettes (eye shadows) alone can range up to $65. Fancy makeup brands like Dior are used mainly by famous models and Hollywood stars.

5. YSL

Yves Saint Laurent has some of the most appealing makeup products on the market. The fashion giant values customer satisfaction, and that is why they make the best makeup products. The YSL foundation has a smooth texture that many love. It also feels light and natural on the face, an element that makes many who can afford the brand buy it more.

6. Anastasia Beverly Hills

Do you notice the eye shadows of your favourite American celebrity? Most of them are from Anastasia Beverly Hills. The pallets cost an excellent $45 and are highly praised by the users. The company has various makeup products like lipsticks, brushes, mascaras, eyebrow gels and liners.

7. L’Oreal

L’Oreal has had quality products year in year out. The prices may be high but worth your money. The company has its headquarters at the heart of Paris, a city of fashion and taste. L'Oreal not only has makeup products but also sells hair products.

8. MAC

This company that was initially founded in Toronto, Canada, has been the epitome of quality make up for decades. MAC products may be readily available for the common makeup enthusiast, but the brand is still as pricy as it was. Consumers like the brand’s products as they are easy to use.

9. Estee Lauder

Estee Lauder has been one of the high-end makeup brands for more than six decades. The brand is loved by many because of its diversity in makeup products. Men and women can easily get skin and health products from this brand. This is commendable as most make up brands focus on female-only products.

10. Artistry

Artistry makeup products enhance your beauty and give you the confidence that you need. The brand was started by a passionate couple about science, nutrition, and healthiness together with beauty. The company uses healthy fruits from Africa and the Mediterranean in their products, making it one of the safest chemical-free make up brands to use.

11. Elizabeth Arden

Elizabeth Arden products make women feel beautiful and confident. The makeup kits may be expensive for the average income earner, but Elizabeth Arden ensures it's everyone's product. This women-oriented brand has been the number one choice for people since the 1910s. If you are looking for high-quality lip colours and mascaras for women, then Elizabeth Arden should be your brand.

12. Oriflame

Oriflame is one of the most expensive cosmetics brands. The company has penetrated different markets and operates in over 60 countries. Some of the best products from this brand are facial cosmetics like foundations, creams, lotions, face masks, powders and compact lip colours.

13. Nu Skin

Nu Skin products are sold in more than 50 countries. Like the brand name suggests, Nu Skin products make your skin feel fresh and new. The company has anti-ageing formulation in their products and use anti-oxidants in most of their facial cosmetics. The brand caters for all types of skins.

14. Smash Box

Smash Box was started in Culver city by two brothers called Dean and Davis in a creative photo studio. Makeup users that are keen on eye products love this brand as it has quality items. The brand’s products are stylish and have unique properties. Makeup lovers that enjoy pigments that are compact and powdery love this brand.

15. Fenty Beauty

Fenty Beauty is one of the youngest yet most expensive cosmetic brands today. Rihanna launched the brand in 2018. The brand is a favourite for many as it caters for women with different skin shades and skin tones. The average Make up kit by Fenty Beauty can cost as much as $50.

Many users get to know about the most expensive makeup brands through their favourite influencers online. Cosmetic brands continue to widen their markets in different countries. The call for diversity has seen big brands include users of all skin shades, promote their products. High-end cosmetics are of high quality and are loved for their longevity.
