Reese Witherspoon talks about her alcohol-fuelled 2013 arrest

Publish date: 2024-09-30

She shocked the world in April 2013 when she was involved in a drunken roadside altercation.

But Reese Witherspoon is now able to talk openly about her infamous antics, which saw her arrested for disorderly conduct after slurring and screaming at a police officer in Atlanta, Georgia when her husband Jim Toth was pulled over under the suspicion of driving while intoxicated.

Much to her embarrassment, a video of the actress' erratic behaviour later emerged online, while her husband was booked for a DUI.

Scroll down for video 

Sheer beauty: Reese Witherspoon wowed first thing on Monday morning at the photocall for her new film Wild at London's The Mayfair Hotel, which was held as part of the 58th BFI London Film Festival

The star believes people have moved on from the incident and hopes it has served to show a more human side to her formerly squeaky clean persona.

According to The Hollywood Reporter, she said during the press conference in London for her new movie Wild on Monday morning: 'I think it was a moment where people realized that I wasn’t exactly what they thought I was.

'I guess maybe we all like to define people by the way the media presents them, and I think that I showed I have a complexity that people didn’t know about.

Dramatic look: The Legally Blonde star was made up with bright red lipstick and shimmery eye shadow 

Demure: The mother-of-three favours a classic, all American style with a twist

'It’s part of human nature. I made a mistake; we all make mistakes. The best you can do is say sorry and learn from it and move on,' she added.

Her new movie might be called Wild, but Reese played it safe in the fashion stakes in a LBD during a photocall held at The Mayfair Hotel on Monday morning as part of the 58th BFI London Film Festival.

The 38-year-old actress looked stunning in the flattering dress, which fell to her knee and boasted a modest neckline.

True story: Reese posed with author Cheryl Strayed, whose memoir Wild was the inspiration behind the film

Group shot: Producer Bruna Papandera and screenwriter Nick Hornby posed alongside Cheryl and Reese 

Three sheer horizontal panels gave the dress an edge, with one of the stripes featuring bright red detailing.

Reese matched her lipstick to the scarlet embroidery, and opted for a shimmery bronze shadow on her eyelids.

She wore her blonde hair slicked back in a ponytail, with her soft wispy fringe framing her face.

Winning smile: Reese's performance in Wild has received positive reviews from critics 

Moving on: The star boasts a squeaky clean reputation, save for her husband Jim's DUI last year

All smiles: The actress was at ease during the press conference, laughing and joking with the crowd

The star left her husband Jim and their two-year-old son Tennessee at home for her flying visit to the English capital.

In her new film, Reese - who also has children Ava, 15, and Deacon, 10, with her ex-husband Ryan Phillippe – stars as troubled divorcee and ex heroin user Cheryl Strayed.

She finds herself lost after the collapse of her marriage and death of her mother, subsequently embarking upon a 1,100 mile solo hike.

Brave: The film is based on Cheryl's own experience of trekking the Pacific Crest Trail solo 

Rainy weather: Reese braved the London drizzle to promote the film, which was directed by Jean-Marc Vallée

New role: The actress could identify with some aspects of Cheryl's life story, including her divorce

Explaining why the role in Wild appealed to her, the Walk The Line star told VOGUE: 'Nobody knows what goes on behind closed doors, but I think there’s a general sense now that I’ve lived a pretty textured life.

'So many of the things that Cheryl goes through in the book I’ve been through, you know? I’ve been married, I’ve been divorced.'

She continued: 'Cheryl has this idea that the things that have happened to you are part of you. There’s something really beautiful about that idea.'

Natural beauty: The 38-year-old actress set off her look with a simple pair of diamond earrings

Firm friends: Cheryl has praised Reese's 'spectacular' and 'relatable' performance in the film
