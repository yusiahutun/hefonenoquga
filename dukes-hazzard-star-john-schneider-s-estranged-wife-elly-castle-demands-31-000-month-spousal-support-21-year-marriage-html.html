Dukes Of Hazzard's John Schneider's wife Elly Castle demands $31k a month

Publish date: 2024-08-19

Dukes Of Hazzard star John Schneider's estranged wife Elly Castle is seeking $31,000 a month in temporary spousal support.

The couple, who have three adult children, were married for 21 years before they split in 2014.

Their divorce remains unsettled and according to TMZ Castle is seeking the monthly sum as a temporary measure while their case makes it's way through the courts.

TMZ reports that she further states that her estranged husband earns more than $83,000 a month.
