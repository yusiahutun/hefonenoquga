Scottie attended the Cardinal Newman High School in West Palm Beach, Florida. After a season, he transferred to NSU University School in Fort Lauderdale, Florida. Towards the end of 2019, he transferred to Montverde Academy in Montverde, Florida.

Career

Barnes' career was colourful from his high school days. While at Cardinal Newman High School, he made it to the All-Area second team and MaxPreps Freshman All-American honours. At NSU University School, He led the team to a City of Palms Classic championship and was named the MVP.

At Montverde, he received All-American first-team honours from MaxPreps and Sports Illustrated. In addition, he was selected to play in the McDonald's All-American Game, Nike Hoop Summit, and Jordan Brand Classic. The three games were cancelled because of the Covid19 pandemic.

He was considered the fourth-best player in the 2020 recruiting class by ESPN. He was highly ranked in his class, and he chose to commit to Florida State. However, he had been sought by the likes of Miami, Kentucky and Oregon.

College career

On March 13, 2021, Barnes registered a career-high 21 points, but his team lost 75-80 to Georgia Tech at the ACC Tournament Championship. During his first year, Scottie Barnes’ Florida State stats averaged:

  • 10.3 points

He was named ACC Freshman of the Year, ACC Sixth Man of the Year and third-team All-ACC Honors. However, his college career ended as he declared for the NBA draft.

Professional career

Barnes was selected with the fourth overall pick by the Toronto Raptors in the 2021 NBA draft. He signed his rookie contract on August 8, 2021, and made his debut for the team on October 20. He registered 12 points, nine rebounds, and one assist, but it wasn't enough to help his team victory against the Washington Wizards.

Two days later, the Raptors benefitted from his first double-double as he registered 25 points and 13 rebounds in a 115-83 win over the Boston Celtics. In January 2022, he recorded a career-high 27 points against the Washington Wizards. A month later, he pushed his limit to 28 points against the Brooklyn Nets.
