Pregnant Shakira shows off her growing belly on outing with partner Gerard Pique

Publish date: 2024-03-01

Her hips don't lie and neither can her belly in her last months of pregnancy.

Shakira did little to cover up her burgeoning tummy last week when she had lunch with her partner Gerard Pique and musician friends in Barcelona.

The Colombian beauty glowed as she revealed a pronounced figure of an expectant mother.

She could be approaching eight months pregnant after a source confirmed she was three months along in late July,Life & Style reports.

The 37-year-old wore baggy jeans, a low-cut loose white top and a multi-color knit sweater jacket. 

She accessorized the look with a black leather hobo bag,  and a simple necklace and a light touch of makeup. Her blonde wavy hair was kept under a wine-colored mountain hat.

Her sportsman partner dressed smartly casual as well, wearing jeans, a black sweater and black sneakers.

Incredibly, the She Wolf singer braved the burden of added pregnancy weight on her feet by opting to wear heels on the outing. 

Perhaps she wanted to add height to her tiny 5'2" frame to her towering 6'4" soccer stud.

Wherever, whenever: The 5'2" Grammy winner is keen to keep up with her 6'4" sportsman; she put more pressure on her pregnant body in a pair of heels

The couple of four years are soon expecting a boy, and already have a son together, Milan, aged one.

On the trip they walked holding hands as they entered a restaurant and met with members of the Mexican rock band Mana for lunch.

After the lunch date, the She Wolf singer glammed up to shoot a music video with the band for their upcoming collaboration Mi Verdad (My Truth), Billboard reports.
