After admitting to her fair share of heartache on The Only Way Is Essex, Chloe Sims has finally found love with new boyfriend Joe Fournier, and it looks like the couple are very relaxed in each others company judging from their antics at the wrap party for Chloe’s reality show.

Chloe joined her Essex co-stars for a glam bash on Wednesday to toast the end of series nine of the hit show, but for Chloe it proved to be a messy end to the night as she spilled out of her skimpy top.

The mum of one was met by personal trainer Joe as she left the party in central London.
