Tom Clare says he is done with Love Island as he opens up on split with Samie

Publish date: 2024-05-02

LOVE Island hunk Tom Clare has told how he is "done" with the show that made him famous.

The footballer, 23 - who recently moved in with co-star Casey O'Gorman, 26 - made the striking comments in an exclusive chat with The Sun.

They came amid speculation Tom is back with his Love Island star ex girlfriend Samie Elishi, 23, after the pair went back to the same hotel following a film premiere.

The 2023 ITV2 show stars - who swiftly became fan favourites - stunned Love Island viewers when they revealed their split in April.

He alluded to their reunion as he told us: "[We're on] really good terms.

"I've got a lot of love and respect for Samie, so yeah it's good."

Yet he had some blunt comments about any return to Love Island's iconic villa in the future.

He told us: "No, It’s not for me that.

"It’s a once in a lifetime experience and I think if I went back in, I don’t know how it would work.

"It’s just not for me, I’ve done it now, best thing I’ve ever done, so I want to keep it that way. I wouldn’t want to go back in and ruin it."

Yet now he has moved down south in a lads pad with Casey.
