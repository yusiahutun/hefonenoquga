Zendaya and Timothe Chalamet Wore Matching Leather Jumpsuits

Publish date: 2024-03-20

Call it double vision: Zendaya and Timothee Chalamet were twinning as they stepped out at a press conference promoting their new film Dune: Part Two.

The actress wore a baby-pink full-body belted leather jumpsuit, while Chalamet donned what appeared to be the exact same style but in baby blue. Both jumpsuits had a motorcycle feel and were bedecked with multiple zippers.

Zendaya’s jumpsuit was styled cheekily off the shoulder on one side, while Chalamet’s was unzipped partway down his chest. Zendaya’s partially unzipped pant leg grazed the top of her matching pink stiletto heels. Chalamet wore more of a baggy-legged look and had matching combat-style boots to accompany his outfit; he also sported a double chain necklace, while Zendaya appeared to wear one solitary ring to enhance the quirky look.

The stars were in Seoul, South Korea, to promote their much anticipated upcoming film, which will be released on March 1st. In the movie, Zendaya plays a warrior named Chani who appeared to Paul (played by Chalamet) in a series of visions in the original 2021 film. Both films are adaptations of a 1965 novel by Frank Herbert.

Zendaya has been on quite a sci-fi fashion kick for the film's press tour, wearing a viral Mugler robot suit just last week. A soft serve-shaped gown, plunging blazer, and Dune-inspired cropped set also made appearances, making this one of her most fashionable tours yet.
