She's still his girl after all these years.

With his good looks and sweet smile, Taylor Lautner could probably have any girl he set his mind on but right now he wants the one that slipped away just as was becoming famous.

The Twilight hunk was spotted on a date with his high school sweetheart Sara Hicks on Wednesday at a Los Angeles Clippers game.

Date night! Taylor Lautner and high school sweetheart are going strong; the pair was spotted leaving Staples Centre in Los Angeles after watching a LA Clippers basketball game

The pair definitely looked smitten as they prepared to leave the Staples Centre in Taylor's luxury vehicle.

Taylor, 20, wore a sheepish grin as he sat behind the wheel revving up the engine.

Pretty Sara, 19, who is also a cheerleader for the LA Clippers basketball team, seemed a little giggly herself as she sat in the leather cushioned passenger seat.

Taylor and Sara were both dressed down and casual for their sporty date, with Taylor bundled up in a blue jacket, brown jeans and white T-shirt, while Sara wore a grey striped knit top and jeans.

Never lost touch: Taylor always stayed in contact with Sara even after their break up about three years ago, and last spring decided to give their romance another go

The couple first dated in high school before the actor found fame in the Twilight film franchise, and broke up about three years ago.

Still, Taylor and Sara remained friends and always stayed in touch, and last spring decided to give their romance another go.

They enjoy seeing movies and going out to dinner, or just hanging out together.

In May of last year, the pair was also spotted on a dinner date in Los Angeles, and gentleman Taylor even opened the car door for Sara as they were leaving.

Dining out: Taylor took Sara out to dinner in Los Angeles on April 30, 2012, and they seemed smitten

She's got cheer: Sara, who is a cheerleader for the LA Clippers, has re-captured Taylor's heart

Now it appears their relationship is back on track.

Taylor is known as a nice guy who typically stays in touch with all of his exes.

The actor had a highly publicised romance with singer Taylor Swift from October to December 2009.

The Valentine's Day co-stars spent many romantic rendezvous together, including a few days in Nashville where Taylor had a home.

But Swift parted ways after the actor's 19th birthday party allegedly because he liked her more than she liked him.

Regrets: Taylor Swift dated the Twilight hunk from October to December 2009, but broke it off only to regret it

The singer's 2010 song Back to December oozes regret, and is probably about her wolf boy as she sings 'I miss your tan skin, your sweet smile.'

Taylor's Abduction co-star Lily Collins, who dated the actor while they were filming the action drama, has only nice things to say about him even though they broke up just days before the September 18, 2011 premiere.

‘He’s a talented actor and a gentleman. Taylor is very humble. He has so many fans and he never forgets that,' Lily told Seventeen magazine.
