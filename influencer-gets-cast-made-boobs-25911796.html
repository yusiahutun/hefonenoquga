Influencer gets a cast made of her boobs as 'celebration' of her chest

Publish date: 2024-02-14

Body positive influencer Nelly London has celebrated her boobs by getting an art cast of them.

Nelly who resides in Brighton often posts uplifting content where she urges her 457,000 Instagram followers to love their stretch marks, cellulite and hip dips - just like she does.

The brunette beauty has also been open about her botched boob job, which left her with permanent scars.

Previously, she old BBC Three .“Actually I now take really great pride in showing off my scars. I take a lot of pictures of them and put them on Instagram. I am openly proud of them because they’ve been such a journey for me.

Nelly still shares the same sentiment - celebrating her boob journey by getting a cast of her prized possessions.

Visiting body casting artist Lydia Reeves, she ditched her clothes ready for the breast casting experience.

Nelly shared the process originally on her Instagram story and has opened up about what inspired her to get the cast done.

She said: “I got my boobs cast!

“I am so glad I got to do this and celebrate my boobies exactly as they are right now.

“The poor gals have really been through it, I think I’ve had 8 or 9 corrective procedures over the past 9 years, and they’re about to go through it again so I wanted to document them right now because they are perfect.”

The influencer had to stand in the same position for 20 minutes with her hands behind her head to let the artist fully paint her boobs.

But Nelly will have to wait for the finished product, as the cast comes in all sorts of colours and patterns - perfect as an ornament for the house!
