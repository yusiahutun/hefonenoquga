Who is Family Ties star Justine Bateman?

Justine Bateman is an American actress, writer, director, and producer. She was born on February 19th, 1966, in Rye, New York, in the United States. She is the daughter of Victoria Elizabeth, a former flight attendant, and Kent Bateman.

Justine's father was an active figure in showbiz. Kent Bateman worked as a writer, producer, and director. On the other hand, her mother was a Pan Am stewardess and is originally from the United Kingdom.

Is Justine Bateman related to Jason Bateman? Yes, she was raised alongside her younger brother, Jason Bateman. He is also active in the entertainment industry and is best known for his impressive role as Michael Bluth on Arrested Development.

Educational background

Justine Bateman attended Taft High School in Woodland Hills, California. She could not join college because of her contractual obligation with Family Ties. However, this did not kill her dream to pursue further studies.

Justine Bateman's professional career

Is Jason Bateman's sister an actress? Yes, Justine's passion for acting was evident right from a young age. She idolised actresses like Jeanne Moreau, Charlotte Rampling, and Anna Magnani. She was equally encouraged by her father and brother, who shared the same passion.

Justine joined Various Studios as an actress, producer, and writer in 1982. The following year, she became part of the TV series Family Ties. She was actively involved in the series until its end in 1989.

In 1986 and 1987, she was nominated for an Emmy in the Outstanding Supporting Actress in a Comedy Series category for her role in Family Ties. Her fascinating acting skills have earned her roles in several movies and television series.
