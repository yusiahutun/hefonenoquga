15 Best Summer Sneakers for Women in 2024

Publish date: 2024-08-26

Best White Sneaker

Isabel Marant Beth Low-Top Sneakers

Best White Sneaker

Isabel Marant Beth Low-Top Sneakers

Credit: Nordstrom

“Clean white sneakers are a summer staple,” Cooper says. “This elevated classic style by Isabel Marant features grip-tape straps, making them easy to slip on and off while you’re on the go this summer. The perforated leather will keep your feet cool.” Style them with cropped trousers and a blouse for a casual workplace, or with a swimsuit and denim cut-offs for a beach weekend.

Sizes: 35–41 EU

Colors: White

Best Trending

Adidas Samba Classic Shoes

Best Trending

Adidas Samba Classic Shoes

Credit: Adidas

Adidas Sambas have been the It sneaker for several years now, and their reign continues into summer 2024. They are a favorite for a reason: These sneakers have an easy, low-top silhouette that looks great with everything from floral dresses to button-downs and jean shorts.

Sizes: 6½–14

Colors: White, black

Best For Hiking

Salomon ACS+ Sneakers

Best For Hiking

Salomon ACS+ Sneakers

Credit: Nordstrom

Whether you have a camping trip on the books or a day hike with friends planned, the proper footwear will be the most important part of your look. Made with heavy-duty rubber soles to help you navigate muddy patches or rocks, these sneakers will make feel supported all day long.

Sizes: 5½–14

Colors: Black, white

Most Fashion-Forward

Chloé Nama Platform Sneakers

Most Fashion-Forward

Chloé Nama Platform Sneakers

“Chloé’s Nama sneaker is the must-have shoe for summer,” Cooper says. “This unique, effortlessly cool style is offered in vibrant color variations and features an eco-conscious design partially constructed from recycled materials.” Signature whipstitching, patchwork detailing, and a platform sole add dimension and a stylish spin to the classic kick.

Most Sustainable

Most Sustainable

Veja’s sneaker is made with materials that are durable and eco-friendly, like Amazonian rubber, sugar cane, recycled polyester and organic cotton. We love this style in white with the steel-blue trim, for an airy palette that’s dreamy and laid-back.

Sizes: 35–41 EU

Best High-Top

Converse Chuck Taylor High–Top Sneakers

Best High-Top

Converse Chuck Taylor High–Top Sneakers

You can’t go wrong with an icon. Converse’s Chuck Taylor high-top sneaker has been around since 1922. Designed originally as a shoe for men’s basketball, they are now wardrobe staples for any occasion. The optic-white colorway is especially perfect for summer days, thanks to a canvas upper that is breathable and lightweight. And don’t worry about them getting dirty—the worn-in look is part of the high-tops’ timeless charm.

Sizes: 5–15

Colors: Optic white and black

Best Splurge

Loewe Flow Runner Mix Leather Sneakers

Best Splurge

Loewe Flow Runner Mix Leather Sneakers

Credit: Saks

“Loewe’s Flow Runner sneakers offer a retro vibe that is perfect for summer,” Cooper says. “This laid-back style is offered in a ton of fun color combinations, as well as lightweight and breathable fabrications including nylon and denim.” Whether worn with sweat shorts for a casual, athletic look or a sundress for something dressier, these sneakers are worth investing in.

Sizes: 5–11

Colors: Shitake, gold, white, and more

Best Slip-On

Vans Classic Slip-Ons

Best Slip-On

Vans Classic Slip-Ons

Credit: Nordstrom

Another sleek and effortless option that never goes out of style is a Vans slip-on sneaker. Completely laceless and made from canvas for breathability, they are perfect for summer. The infamous checkerboard pattern adds a fun styling twist to any basic bikini, denim bottom, or miniskirt.

Sizes: 5–11

Colors: White, black checker

Best Street-Style-Approved

Best Street-Style-Approved

The low-top shape is made with a lace-up design, and fabricated in leather upper with a rubber sole for traction, no matter your destination. Try the white colorway for a shoe that transitions well from city bodega runs to long walks on the beach.

Best Sporty Shoe

Nike V2K Run Sneakers

Best Sporty Shoe

Nike V2K Run Sneakers

Credit: Nordstrom

Tap into the Nike archive with a Y2K-inspired shoe silhouette that’s been modernized for today. The iconic swoosh across the sides brings a graphic punch to the overall minimalist style of the white leather shoe. Pair with a ribbed tube sock and bare leg for an athletic, on-trend feel.

Best for the Beach

Keds Champion Core Sneakers

Best for the Beach

Keds Champion Core Sneakers

One of Keds’ most beloved styles, the Champion sneaker has been in the brand’s repertoire for decades. Still made with a rubber sole and a cushioned footbed for all-day comfort, this version swaps the traditional canvas upper for white leather. Lean into a ’90s minimalist-prep vibe by pairing them with a linen button-down and pleat-front trouser shorts.

Sizes: 4–12

Colors: White
