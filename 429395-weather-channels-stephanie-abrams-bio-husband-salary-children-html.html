Early life

The TV personality was born on October 27th, 1978, in Wellington, Florida, USA. Her parents divorced when she was still young. Stephanie Abrams' family lived in West Palm Beach.

The enrolled at the University of Florida in Gainesville. At the university, she pursued a Bachelor of Science in Geography with a Minor in Mathematics. She graduated with honours and was elected to Phi Beta Kappa. She later went to Florida State University in Tallahassee to pursue her second degree, studying Bachelor of Science in meteorology.
