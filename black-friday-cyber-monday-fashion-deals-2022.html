All the Extended Cyber Monday 2022 Fashion Deals Worth Shopping

Publish date: 2024-07-20

Black Friday and Cyber Monday, an annual invitation to shop (and shop, and shop) some of the year's best fashion deals, are officially over. But as the editor tasked with curating BAZAAR's top shopping guides, I know from experience that the best fashion brands will give us an extension to shop their top deals.

This year, Black Friday and Cyber Monday fashion deals are still going strong after their end dates. In extended Cyber Monday deals, happening right now, you can still find otherwise-unseen savings on everything from winter sweaters to puffer jackets to holiday party dresses, plus timely fashion gifts for everyone on your list. With some extra time to shop, we're picking up Cyber Monday fashion deals at Nordstrom, Net-a-Porter, Saks Fifth Avenue, Shopbop, and more on the department store side. Individual brands are also getting in on the extra-long Cyber Monday fashion sales, from 50 percent off Mansur Gavriel's leather bags to up to 70 percent off on holiday sweaters and outerwear at & Other Stories.

Another day to shop is a treat. With so many good deals, you can spend 24 hours browsing and still not see every single deal. And, today is Giving Tuesday: At many places, a portion of the proceeds from your order will go to a charitable cause.

Ahead, I've listed the 87 best extended Cyber Monday fashion sales worth shopping before time to shop the sales actually runs out. (I would know—I've put in the hours to hand-select each one.) I'll add more of the best Cyber Monday 2022 fashion deals, and the top pieces to shop from each sale, as the official deals continue rolling in.
