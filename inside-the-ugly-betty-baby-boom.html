Inside the Ugly Betty Baby Boom

Publish date: 2024-04-11

It’s baby talk all the time now on the Ugly Betty set.

Mark Indelicato tells PEOPLE that everyone couldn’t wait for a first look at costar Rebecca Romjin‘s 6-week-old twins with husband Jerry O’Connell.

“We got pictures of Dolly and Charlie,” Indelicato says at the Cinema Society screening of He’s Just Not That Into You. “They are beautiful. Charlie is literally bigger than Dolly. Charlie’s face is round and shmushy.”

Of course, Jerry and Rebecca’s twins should prepare for some competition for attention since costar Ana Ortiz is expecting her own bundle of joy.

So how did the cast find out about Ortiz’s happy news? “We were at a cast party,” explains Indelicato. “Everyone went into the cigar room. Ana didn’t go and that’s so not like her.”

Indelicato adds “She’s starting to show now. And we’re turning her dressing room into a little nursery. I’m so excited for her baby.” – Reporting by Jeffrey Slonim
