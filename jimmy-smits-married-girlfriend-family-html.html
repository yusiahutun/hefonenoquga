Marriage & Children

American actor Jimmy Smits has lived his share of a well-running married life. He was happily wedded to his high school sweetheart, Barbara Smits.

Jimmy and his wife had their wedding on 10 June 1980; however, the two had already started a family long before their marriage.

While the actor was only 19, Barbara had given birth to their daughter Taina Smits.

Likewise, the birth of the couple's son Kino Smits four year after their marriage only added to their happiness. Unfortunately though, on 22nd June, the couple separated officially.

Years have passed since the split and today; his ex-wife is no longer alive. Jimmy's ex-partner Barbara lost her life to pneumonia on 16th April 2019.

But still today, many are left wondering what had gone wrong in the Star Wars actor's married life all of a sudden. However, looking at the incidents following their divorce, the reasons are apparent.
