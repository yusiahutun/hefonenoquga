Zaha's social media post hours before Palace contract expires has fans scratching heads

Publish date: 2024-04-28

Wilfried Zaha has left fans scratching their heads with a cryptic message just 24 hours before his Crystal Palace contract expired.

The wing wizard shared a snap of Palace’s training ground in Beckenham, Greater London on his social media. And Palace fans have been left confused, with the 30-year-old looking destined to leave Selhurst Park this summer.

Zaha, who is currently paid £200,000-per-week, will become a free agent on Friday - when his contract expires. And the Ivorian hasn't been short of offers, with Lazio, Fenerbahce, and an unnamed Saudi Arabian side all interested in his services.

There have even been links to Paris Saint-Germain, with the French giants implausibly seeing Zaha as a replacement for Lionel Messi. PSG lost their Argentine star to Inter Miami this summer, but there has been no approach towards the Palace man.

Zaha has come through Crystal Palace's academy - and he briefly moved away to Manchester United in 2013. However, Zaha failed to make the grade at United, and within a couple of seasons, he was back at Selhurst Park.

The attacker has remained in London ever since. Palace fans had hoped he would remain at Selhurst Park - but Zaha's future remains unclear.

Zaha has recently teamed up with rapper Stormzy to take control of non-league side AFC Croydon Athletic in their hometown - as part of a consortium.

A statement said: "The consortium will own, operate and develop their childhood hometown football club... They hope to take the entire community on this exciting journey with them." And Zaha added his own words: "May the journey begin!"
