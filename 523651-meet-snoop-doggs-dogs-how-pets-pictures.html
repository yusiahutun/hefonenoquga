Meet Snoop Dogg's dogs: How many pets does he have? (pictures)

Publish date: 2024-08-12

Snoop Dogg is an iconic American rapper and entertainer. He is among the most successful rappers of this generation thanks to his iconic style and lyrical talent. From his breakout collaborations to ventures in film and business, Snoop's career is a testimony of his lasting impact on pop culture. But aside from his profession, the rapper is famous for his love for animals, especially dogs. But how many does he have? And what breeds are Snoop Dogg's dogs?

Snoop's deep affection for hounds is evident in his stage name and active support for animal welfare initiatives. He owns a famous clothing label for pets and organises and participates in various dog charity events. Snoop often shares heartwarming moments with his dogs on social media, demonstrating his love for his pets and the joy they bring into his life.

Snoop Dogg's dogs

How many dogs does Snoop Dogg own? According to US Magazine, the rapper takes care of 11 pet canines (as of October 2023). His dogs comprise different breeds, which he has shared images and videos of on social media. Below is a breakdown of some of Snoop Dogg's dogs (at the time of writing).

Juelz – French bulldog

Juelz Broadus is a French bulldog adopted by Snoop in November 2014. The pup was reportedly a gift from fellow rapper Wiz Khalifa. Juelz has its own Instagram account, opened to him by his master Snoop in May 2015. In addition, the pup is often seen backstage with his owner, accompanying him to some of his shows.

Lil Snoop – Pitbull

The rapper also owns a Pitbull, reportedly named Lil Snoop. He was first seen online on 13 June 2020 after his owner posted a photo of the pair bonding with the caption:

I love my dog, and he loves his dogg ✨

Frank – French bulldog

Frank is another French bulldog owned by the rapper. On 30 January 2022, Frank went missing from his home in South LA, prompting a plea for help from his owner, Snoop. In an Instagram post, the rapper uploaded a poster with Franks details, revealing his breed as a grey stripe French bulldog. Frank was later found by a couple who returned him home.

Though Snoop owns 11 hounds, information about his other breeds is unknown apart from their names. According to DogTime, the rapper shared some of his pup's names in a past interview. He mentioned Black Mamba, Petey, Dirty Red, Chris Mo'Cream, Jewels, and TayTay.

What kinds of dogs does Snoop Dogg have?

According to Celebrity Pets, the rapper has various canine breeds. His collections include French bulldogs(Juelz and Frank), Pitbulls (Mamba and Lil Snoop), a Labradoodle and a Cane Corso.

Snoop Dogg's pet products

The American rapper co-founded a pet line store, Snoop Doggie Dogg, in 2022. According to People, the rapper partnered with Little Earth Productions Inc. to create his pet line store, launching it on 17 November 2022. The rapper designs the products available.

Snoop Dogg's dog clothes

According to the official store, featured products include apparel, toys, accessories and bowls. Also available are assorted canine clothing consisting of halftime jerseys, hoodies, pet pyjamas, pet t-shirts and bandanas.
