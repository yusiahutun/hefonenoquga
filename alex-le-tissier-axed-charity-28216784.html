Alex Le Tissier axed as charity ambassador over leaked sex tape despite raising 10k

“The reasoning wasn’t totally clear but my leaked sex tape was mentioned and someone had linked the tape link into the comity group.”

Alex feels judged for being on OnlyFans and says she's lost out on other work too.

She added: “I feel that a lot of people are targeting me due to my new success and I feel it’s unfair to be punished or judged on my leaked sex tape. I never wanted that to be out in the public.

“I was also dropped by a football podcast I did every month for the same reasons. I guess it’s the price you pay for doing well in the porn industry.

“I feel it’s strange people cannot separate the two. My job has absolutely nothing to do with charity. And I find it disturbing that people want to link my profession with children.

“I was so proud to be an ambassador and it shows we have a long long long way to go with the stigma we face.”

In August, the 30-year-old exclusively told us her sex tape with husband Mitch was leaked on the internet.

It left her fearing for her kids and she described how she “immediately felt sick” after finding out.

She had decided to join OnlyFans in lockdown after struggling to feed her kids during the pandemic.

Alex later bagged a job at Babestation but her dramatic switch to sex work resulted in her claiming Matt Le Tissier had brandished her a “bad mum”.

Now, writing on Twitter about her latest setback, she said: “I’m disappointed to announce I have been asked to step down as an ambassador for Southampton Doing It For The Kids.

“I ran over 130 miles in five days, put my body through pain and raised more than £10,000 for two amazing children’s charities last year.

“Other people's opinions of me based on my chosen career will not change my passion to try to help the less fortunate in the city of Southampton.

“Whilst I won’t officially be a part of the charity going forward, I’ll continue to support in any way I can, including hosting our annual charity ALT football tournament.

“I would like to thank Tony and Tracy for giving me that role in the first place.”

And in a show of support, one mum with two disabled children wrote: “Not many people in society actually care about those with disabilities, and my eldest has faced a fair amount of discrimination due to how severe his autism is.

“I think it’s disgusting you have raised so much money which families like myself deeply appreciate any help we can get, and they ask you to step down.

“I’m actually shocked. As a special needs parent we need more people like you that actually care about our children. I wish there were more people like you.”

Alex also told the Daily Star other parents have contacted her expressing “how upset” they are about the decision.

And after her sex tape was leaked earlier this year, Alex said: “It is disgusting in my opinion.

“I know a lot of people will read this and think I've put myself in that position but men and women need to understand the meaning of consent.

“If you subscribe to my OnlyFans that's a mutual agreement of consent. You are allowed to enjoy those pictures and videos and whatever you purchase for yourself. That doesn’t mean share with the world.

“No one deserves for their content to be leaked and it’s massively misogynistic. Some cannot stand other people being successful and happy.”

A statement from Southampton Doing It For The Kids in response to this story read: "Alex was appointed some time ago as an Ambassador of our small charity run by a committee of volunteers, certainly before she entered in to what is referred to as her current "profession", unfortunately over recent months those volunteers have found themselves, for an unreasonable amount of their time, explaining her appointment to those we rely upon for funding, this was due to, what we believed to be, negative exposure on local and national media including but not exclusive to inappropriate comments on social media upon the passing of our late Queen.

"Communications were entered in to with Alex at the time culminating in the committee requesting she consider stepping down as Ambassador. We take this opportunity to thank Alex for her and Mitch's fund raising referred to as we were beneficiaries of a percentage of the money raised by her charity run.

"Southampton Doing It For The Kids will continue to focus on providing much needed support to local families facilitated by the fantastic fund raising of committee members, companies and individuals across our city."
