Hailey Bieber's Latest Latte Look Proved That Brown Is the New Black

Publish date: 2024-08-11

The It girls’ love for brown this autumn is well-documented, with an almost infinite number of chocolate-covered street-style looks on everyone's feeds. Between Megan Fox doubling down on the monochromatic brown trend and Taylor Swift’s cocoa cozy 'fit, brown clothing — once considered a neutral in your wardrobe — is now the color to have on rotation. And the latest cool girl to co-sign shades of brown for fall is none other than Hailey Bieber.

On Wednesday, the Rhode beauty founder stepped out for a girl's night with Zoë Kravitz at Sushi Park. a celeb-beloved dinner spot in West Hollywood. Proving chocolate brown is the biggest color trend (following shortly behind crimson red), Hailey sported an all-brown ‘fit comprised of an oversized Saint Laurent chocolate brown jacket layered over a coordinating knitted crewneck sweater and paired with a matching suede micro miniskirt. A pair of black penny loafers, white tube socks, and a black handbag provided the finishing touches to her latte-inspired look.

Hailey finished the look by sporting a matching dark brown manicure and a simple, no-makeup makeup glam complete with a bronzy complexion and a glossy lip, and she wore her chin-grazing brunette bob down with tousled waves and a middle part.

Like always, Hailey was ahead of everyone when it came to the brown-on-brown-on-brown trend. Back in October, the model attended Kim Kardashian's 43rd birthday party at Funke restaurant in Beverly Hills wearing a brown leather micro-miniskirt and a coordinating, oversized coffee-colored blazer with a white T-shirt underneath. Deep red slingback kitten heels, a matching handbag, her signature "B" diamond-encrusted necklace, and chunky gold earrings rounded out her 'fit.
